Pam &amp; Tommy star Lily James wore out 50 pairs of fake breasts

ACTRESS Lily James boobed repeatedly while playing Pamela Anderson — as she wore out 50 pairs of fake breasts.

The Brit, 32, was given a new set of the gel-filled bosoms every day while filming the new TV series Pam & Tommy.

She had to spend four hours in hair and make-up to look like the Baywatch star for the Disney+ show, which also stars Sebastian Stan as Pam’s lover Tommy Lee.

The couple are seen romping throughout — and repeated friction meant the props department had to provide a regular supply of replacement boobs.

The show’s head of make-up, Jason Collins, said: “They’re made out of gel-filled silicone appliances, which move like real skin and absorb light similarly. We used a new pair every day for filming which meant having 50 made.”

Make-up artists also used a fake forehead to increase the distance between Lily’s eyebrows and hairline. Jason added: “We went through 65 to 70 foreheads throughout shooting.”

Lily spent four months of filming speaking like Canadian Pamela and had to go on long walks at home to get her English accent back. 

She said it was harder than for other roles, adding: “It was more glaringly obvious I did have to actively take myself off and go to the English countryside and go on long walks and assimilate back into myself.”
