Who is Taron Egerton? 

Taron Egerton is a Welsh model and actor who shot to stardom with his portrayal of Elton John in Dexter Fletcher’s 2019 film the Rocketman.

Unlike Bohemian Rhapsody, the Queen biopic where star Rami Malek lip-synched to Freddie Mercury’s original vocal tracks, Egerton sang his own parts, a decision showcased on the Giles Martin-directed soundtrack for Rocketman.

The Elton John film was the first time Egerton was identified as a singer.

He is the recipient of a Golden Globe Award and has received nominations for a Grammy Award and two British Academy Film Awards.

Early Life of Taron Egerton

Taron Egerton was born on 10 November 1989. He is 33 years of age as of 2022. His zodiac sign is Scorpio.

His birthplace is Birkenhead, Merseyside, United Kingdom. He holds a Welsh nationality and belongs to the white ethnic group.

He was born and raised in a Christian household and is a believer in Christianity. Taron Egerton’s mother’s name is Christine Egerton, and she is a social worker.

His father operated a bed-and-breakfast near Liverpool.

Taron was raised by his mother after his parents got divorced when he was 2 years of age.

He also has two half-siblings. His younger brothers’ names are Mary Egerton and Rosie Egerton.

He relocated to Anglesey where grew up and spent his childhood. He then relocated to Aberystwyth at the age of 12. He currently resides in London, United Kingdom.

As far as his education is concerned, Taron had his early schooling at a local elementary school in Birkenhead.

He then enrolled his name at Ysgol Penglais Comprehensive school which is situated in Aberystwyth, Ceredigion, West Wales.

After studying there, he went to RADA (Royal Academy of Dramatic Art) and pursued to learn acting. He completed his course with BA (Hons) in Acting in 2012.

Taron Egerton Career 

Taron was first seen in the role of James Kent’s 2014 drama film, Testament of Youth. The film was based on Vera Brittain’s novel of the same name.

The film included an ensemble cast of Dominic West, Miranda Richardson, Alicia Vikander, Kit Harington, Colin Morgan, Charlotte Hope, Jonathan Bailey, Alexandra Roach, Emily Watson, Hayley Atwell, Anna Chancellor, and Joanna Scanlan.

He reprised the role of Gary “Eggsy” Unwin in the 2017 sequel of the film, Kingsman: The Golden Circle.

Taron got the pivotal role of his career when he was cast in Dexter Fletcher’s musical biopic, Rocketman where he portrayed the role of Elton John.

Taron has received the honorary Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Motion Picture Musical or Comedy and also received numerous other accolades including a BAFTA nomination.

Taron has been part of many other recognizable films and television shows such as Billionaire Boys Club (2018), The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance (2019), Watership Down (2018), Eddie the Eagle (2016), Legend (2015), Lewis (2013).

He was also seen in the autobiographical television series, In with the Devil and in Jon S. Baird’s biographical drama film, Tetris.

Beyond his screen and stage acting, Egerton has lent his voice to audiobooks. In 2019, to correspond with the release of “Rocketman,” he served as the narrator for the audiobook of Elton John’s autobiography “Me.”

The next year, Egerton voiced John Constantine in the audiobook series of Neil Gaiman’s “The Sandman.”
