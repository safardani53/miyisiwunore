Who Is Emily Arnett, Zach Arnett Wife? Married Life &amp; Kids

Zachary Arnett is an American football coach. As of now, he is the head coach at Mississippi State University.

Zach, born on September 10, 1986, in Albuquerque, New Mexico, began his athletic journey at La Cueva High School, where he participated in both football and baseball.

His talent and dedication led him to earn a football scholarship at the University of New Mexico, where he further developed his skills as a linebacker.

In 2011, Zach began coaching at San Diego State as a graduate assistant. Over the years, he held various assistant coaching positions at the university, contributing to his growth as a coach.

After a brief period at Syracuse, Zach took on the defensive coordinator role at Mississippi State in January 2020.

Finally, on December 15, 2022, Zach reached a significant milestone in his career when he was appointed the 35th head football coach for the Bulldogs.
