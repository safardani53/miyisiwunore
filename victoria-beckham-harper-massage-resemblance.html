VICTORIA Beckham treated her nine-year-old daughter Harper to a "mummy and me" massage, with fans sharing their shock at the little girl's resemblance to her famous mum.

The 46-year-old star shared a sweet glimpse at their quality family time on Instagram this evening.

In the intimate snap, Harper is laying on a towel with another over her back.

The little girl is facing the camera but appears to be fast asleep, with one hand curled against her face.

Harper's brown hair is swept over one ear, with a small gold hoop earring on display.

Victoria captioned the sweet photo: "Mummy and me massage with my baby girl, kisses #HarperSeven x".

Fans were blown away by how much Harper looked like the former Posh Spice in the snap, particularly with her full lips and button nose.

One follower commented: "She looks so much like you here, it's the mouth and nose x".

Another added: "So sweet. She is your mini ❤️".

A third said: "She looks so much like you ❤️ Mummy's double."

The luxury treat comes shortly after Harper's ninth birthday, where she was spoilt rotten by her famous parents and older siblings; Brooklyn, 21, Romeo, 17, and 15-year-old Cruz.

The family wore personalised T-Shirts with the birthday girl's face on them, and treated Harper to a fun party as well as gushing social media tributes.
