Lil Moco Biography

Lil Moco is a YouTuber mostly known for uploading videos related to challenges, pranks, parody, and reactions. His self-titled YouTube channel has gained more than 2.2 million subscribers and more than 323 million views as of 2019.

Early Life & Family

Lil Moco was born on November 2, 1994, in Zimbabwe, Africa, and was raised there by his parents whose names have not been disclosed.

As for his siblings, he has a sister and a younger brother who often appears in his videos. He is of African nationality and belongs to white ethnicity.

Information about his educational background and childhood have not been revealed. It's unclear where he studied and when he completed his graduation.

Career

Lil Moco launched his self0-titled Youtube channel on February 12, 2010, and uploaded his first video on the channel, , on May 28, 2010. He was 16 years old at the time.

He has since uploaded numerous reaction, comedy and music parody videos on his youtube channel. The parody of music videos is strange and very particular in nature.

He has made parody music videos of various legendary music artists such as Cardi B, Drake, Camila Cabello, Lil Pump and Rihanna. His parody videos have been appreciated by his millions of viewers.

He has now 2.2 million subscribers on his youtube channel and the number of his subscribers clearly shows how much he is adored by his fans. His funny videos have created a huge supportive fanbase for him.

He has uploaded more than 158 videos which have earned more than 355 million views till now. Of course, he is well-appreciated by his fans who enjoy his unique blend of contents.

Net Worth

Lil Moco successfully accumulates the proper number of views which ultimately helps in earning money from his videos. He earns most of his money from his self-titled YouTube channel.

According to www.gazettereview.com, Lil Moco has an estimated net worth of approx. $190,000.

According to the Social Blade website, he earns $1.8K to $28.4K per month and $21.3K to $341.2K per year from his YouTube channel.

Details about his house and cars are not available. However, in August 2013, he had posted a funny video titled in which he joked that he had owned a car shop which he named Moco's Car Shop.

Personal Life: Engaged to Aury Martinez, Children

Moving towards his personal life, Lil Moco isn’t married but is engaged with his loving girlfriend Aury Martinez. The couple shares a beautiful daughter Selena Rose aka Lil Mocosa who was born in August 2016.

It's unknown when Moco first met Aury and started dating. He has not opened up about his past affairs and relationships in any of his videos.
