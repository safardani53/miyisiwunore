Elin Nordegren Goes on Dinner Date With Swedish Hockey Player Douglas Murray in Miami

Elin Nordegren‘s new mansion may not be a bachelorette pad for very long!

The New York Daily News reports Tiger Woods‘ ex-wife enjoyed a dinner date with Swedish hockey player Douglas Murray in Miami on Dec. 7.

Nordegren, 32, was spotted dining with the athlete and two of her siblings at high-end eatery Mr. Chow. After the meal, the former model reportedly headed to Hyde nightclub to keep the evening going.

According to the paper, the single mom also went out in Miami the following night with a group of friends to Casa Tua.

In July, Murray, 32, denied rumors that he was dating Nordegren. The athlete told Swedish tabloid Expressen, “We are not dating and we have never dated.”

Indeed, Nordegren seems to be enjoying the single life. After settling her divorce from Woods in 2010 for $100 million, she purchased a North Palm Beach estate for $12.2 million — only to have it demolished to build her dream multi-million-dollar mansion.

The 21,000-square-foot mansion on the Florida beachfront property is still under construction, but will feature an Olympic pool, koi ponds, and a spa. Nordgren, who shares two children, daughter Sam, 5, and son Charlie, 3, with her golf pro ex, is proudly waving a Swedish flag on the property of her new home.

Woods, 36, also seems to be enjoying life post-divorce. The golfer was seen partying the night away with some attractive female partygoers at California’s Westlake Village Inn on Dec. 1.
