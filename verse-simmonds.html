Career

Verse began working on his own solo songs in 2009 and signed with Cloud 9 and Raydar Management as a solo artist. Via June 30, 2009, he released his debut solo single, Buy You a Round, on Interscope (Up and Down). It eventually reached number 55 on the Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs list and number 63 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Airplay chart.

Simmonds also collaborated with Raydar, Cloud9, Darkchild, and DJ Trauma on the August 2009 mixtape The Sextape Chronicles. The same year, his song was featured on R. Kelly’s album Untitled. In January 2011, he joined Konvict Muzik as a singer-songwriter and producer. In April 2012, Dj Drama presented the rapper’s mixtape Sex Love & Hip Hop, which included Yo Gotti, Kelly Rowland, Snoop Dogg, and Akon.
