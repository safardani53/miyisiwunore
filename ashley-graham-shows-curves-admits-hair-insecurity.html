MODEL Ashley Graham has questioned a darker hair color after previously opening up about postpartum hair loss.

As the filter generated her voice to sound like an echo, Ashley sang into the animated microphone to question her hair color.

"Do I, do I like the dark hair, or do I feel like Elvira?" she joked, comparing herself to the horror host of Elvira: Mistress of the Dark.

HAIR GROWTH JOURNEY

The mom has had quite an experience with her hair in the past, previously detailing the trauma of losing her hair after giving birth to Isaac.

In an interview with Parents, she revealed her entire hairline had fallen out after welcoming her son in January 2020.

"That was more traumatic than even birth because I was like, 'My hair's falling out in clumps — what am I doing?' and then I realized it's actually a thing," she explained.

The model kept fans updated with her hair regrowth journey on Instagram, even making a joke out of it despite its traumatic effect.

"I may not be a bond girl but i can be a bond villain (postpartum baby hairs come through!!!) [sic]," she wrote in a post.

A BRAND NEW LOOK

Last month, the 36-year-old teased fans as she posted an Instagram Story to show that she had changed her hair color.

She posted a selfie of half her face, which showed curly blond hair falling down her shoulder.

The model's eye had heavy eyeliner but nothing else was in show apart from a white robe on her shoulder.

The star didn't add any captions or comments to the post, leaving fans to speculate.

However, she has continued to share photos with her signature brunette locks on her feed.
