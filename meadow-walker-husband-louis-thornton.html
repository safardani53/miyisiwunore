Who is Meadow Walker's estranged husband, Louis Thornton-Allan?

EIGHT years after Paul Walker's death, his only daughter, Meadow, tied the knot with Louis Thornton-Allan.

However, the former couple have decided to call it quits.

Who is Meadow Walker's estranged husband, Louis Thornton-Allan?

Like both Paul and Meadow Walker, Louis Thornton-Allan is an actor.

Hailing from London, England, he relocated to New York City to pursue a career in filmmaking.

An alumni of the Stella Adler Studio, Louis graduated from the institute in 2022.

On October 22, 2021, Meadow and Louis tied the knot at their wedding ceremony in the Dominican Republic.

In place of her late father Paul, Meadow was given away by her godfather, Vin Diesel.

At the time, she told Vogue: "We couldn't have imagined it to be any more perfect and personal.

"And honestly, it was easy and simple. Louis and I knew exactly what we wanted from the start. It was a very intimate celebration.

"It was so sentimental and sweet. I wouldn’t have wanted it any other way."

When did Meadow and Louis split?

On December 28, 2023, Meadow and Louis announced their separation.

In a joint statement, they told their Instagram followers: "After three wonderful years of marriage, we have come to the agreement to amicably separate.

"This is truly a united decision and we sincerely hope that everyone can respect our wishes for privacy.

"We maintain mutual love and respect for one another, and will continue to support each other."

When did they start dating?

In July 2021, Meadow and Louis went Instagram official and revealed they were dating.

By August 7, 2021, the two announced their engagement.
