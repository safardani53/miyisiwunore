What Did He Do Now??

It’s been quite the year for Justin Bieber’s hair.

The singer has changed it up numerous times already in 2016, and it seems he just can’t settle on a style he likes.

At the beginning of the year, he rocked this long-on-top, short-on-the-sides do and sported a man bun.

Then the strands grew into a tangled mess and he dyed it purple.

After tiring of that, he completely freaked out fans by getting dreadlocks, an oh-so controversial decision that he later defended.

But it doesn’t stop there, and so far we’re only in April!

For his next coiffure du jour, the Biebs cut off all the dreads and went with a buzz cut, as depicted above, and teenage girls fell back in love.

That leaves us here, at present day. 

On June 4, 2016, we present to you the 2016 Justin Bieber Hairdo #5:

Platinum again!

Our minds are blown, and we’re not sure if it’s because of how nicely the color complements his skin tone or that we actually dedicated an entire post to Justin Bieber’s hair.

But, hey, you clicked for a reason.

It’s certainly not as bad as when fans losts their minds upon hearing of Justin’s face tattoo, though.

I mean, hair grows, but let’s be real, his face is what catapulted him to stardom.

Maybe his singing and dancing had a little to do with it. And his abs.
