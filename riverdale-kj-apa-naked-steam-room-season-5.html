The season of the CW teen drama will bring the hit Archie Comics-inspired series into a new territory, as the early episodes will focus on the teenage characters experiencing high school prom and graduation.

Back in May, the show's boss explained what they had been able to film before the coronavirus halted proceedings.

Roberto explained: "It's funny, we had shot almost two-thirds of episode 20, which was going to be our prom episode. And we looked at that footage and tried to see if there was a way we could cobble together that episode.

"We actually had shot the prom, where a lot of very dramatic things happen, but we still hadn't shot some big character moments between Archie and Veronica and Betty and Jughead. So we kind of just couldn't do that."
