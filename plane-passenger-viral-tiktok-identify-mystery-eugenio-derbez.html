A PLANE passenger has gone viral on TikTok after she asked viewers to help her identify a mystery celebrity on her flight.

The video got so much attention it even gained a response from the star himself.

Influencer Paige Craig boarded a flight to Los Angeles, California, from Orlando, Flordia, last week when she noticed people kept asking for photos with a fellow passenger in her row.

She had a feeling he was someone famous but couldn't figure out who he was.

She hoped the internet could help her out, so she took a quick clip of the star and uploaded it to TikTok.

Craig wrote in the video: “Can someone tell me who this man is because people keep taking pics with him and I’m too scared to ask him who he is.”

The clip began with the camera focusing on her face before she slowly turned it to show a man with brown hair and glasses sitting next to her.

"Other information - he speaks Spanish and is a very nice man. Thank you for your service," she added in the caption.

In just a few days the video got over 30 million views as people flocked to the comments to help identify the mystery man.

The celeb was soon identified as Mexican actor and director Eugenio González Derbez.

The star has appeared in a number of movies including Overboard, The Valet, Dora and The Lost City Of Gold.

Although Craig went viral for the video, she revealed to Insider that she considered deleting it after she discovered who it was.

“I was contemplating taking it down since I had my answer, and then I wouldn’t have to risk the poor man finding out I did this when I had to sit next to him for five-ish more hours,” Craig said.

“I spent the rest of the five hours stressing that my video would pop up on his phone.”

Luckily for the influencer, he didn't show any hints that he saw the video while they were on the flight.

But by the next day, Derbez responded with a TikTok video of his own.

"Hi Paige, let me introduce myself. My name is Eugenio Derbez and I'm basically an actor and director."

After his introduction, the star shared a montage of his most iconic roles.

"Now, can someone tell me who this girl is?" he jokingly asked. "Because people keep following her, she has more than one million followers on TikTok."

When it came to Derbez's response, Paige thought it was "hilarious," adding to Insider: "It was also nice knowing he wasn't going to put a restraining order on me for the creeper vibes."
