Jack Osbourne & Aree Gearhart

The Fright Club star, 36, revealed on Instagram on Dec. 30 that he popped the question to his girlfriend of over two years, Aree Gearhart.

"Today I asked the most beautiful and loving woman I've ever met to marry me. She said yes!!" he captioned a selfie of himself and his fiancée, who showed off her huge sparkler.

"Life is a series of doors and I'm so excited to walk through this one with her. She's truly a magical being with a heart bigger than anything I could have imagined," Osbourne continued, adding, "Her step-mom skills make my heart so full. I couldn't be happier than I am right now."

Osbourne shares children Pearl Clementine, 9, Andy Rose, 6, and Minnie Theodora, 3, with ex-wife Lisa Stelly. Their divorce was finalized in March 2019.

The HGTV star is engaged to the Michelin-star chef, she revealed on Dec. 28.

"I said absolutely," Soto captioned the shots of the seaside proposal in Carmel-by-the-Sea, California.

This will be Sheremet's third marriage. The former backup dancer shares son Atlas Wilding, 3, with his ex-fiancée, filmmaker Vanessa Black, and was previously married to singer LeAnn Rimes for seven years, and photographer Sarah Silver for five years.

This is the first marriage for Soto, who shares daughter Olivia Gray, 6, with her former long-term boyfriend Steve Grevemberg.

"Deciding to end our engagement last year was heart-wrenching," she wrote in an essay for Today.com. "I used to say, 'It literally takes my breath away to picture my life without him.' But our relationship couldn't stay the way it was. We needed a pause. A long pause. And we took it."

Now, Martin said that she and her man "laugh all the time" together, and look forward to tying the knot.

"It took me 45 years to find my fairy tale. When I realized I was confident on my own, that's when I knew Erik and I were ready to get back together," she wrote in her post. "The realistic version of my fairy tale was ready to be written."
