GEMMA Collins hits back at online trolls that mocked her for wearing a shoulder pad dress, telling her haters she doesn’t have to answer to anyone.

The Towie star, 37, became the subject of online abuse when she wore the outfit to an ITV party – but insists she’s still in love with the dress that sparked mass online attention.

In an exclusive interview, she said: “I am Gemma Collins. I don’t have to answer to anyone about my fashion choices.

“I loved that dress – I wanted to express myself that night and I did exactly that.

“I can take the mick out of myself so whatever anyone has got to say about me I still find it funny. I even loved the memes.

"You will absolutely see more of that in the future.

"If Céline Dion can rock it then why can't the GC?"

The reality star sent the internet into overdrive when she attended ITV's summer party wearing the eye-catching dress.
