WOAH, JENNA!

Meanwhile, Hoda mocked Jenna after she made an NSFW remark live on air.

The awkward moment happened on Thursday during the pair's talk show, Today with Hoda & Jenna.

It all started when the duo was discussing Gwyneth Paltrow's buzzworthy interview on the Call Her Daddy podcast earlier this week.

At one point during the sit-down, host Alex Cooper asked Gwyneth, 50, to compare sexual experiences with her two A-list exes, Ben Affleck, 50, and Brad Pitt, 59.

Listeners were shocked as the actress didn't hold back in revealing what she really thought about her former lovers' performances.

"That is very hard," she began as she pondered how to respond.

But the words soon came out easily for the Goop founder as she continued: "Brad was like this sort of major chemistry love of your life at the time, you know?

"And then, like, Ben was like, you know, technically excellent," Gwyneth admitted to Alex, who broke out in laughter.

JENNA'S CONFESSION

However, Hoda, 58, and Jenna, 41, didn't know what to think of the Iron Man star's confessions, as they both had stunned expressions on their faces.

Upon gathering their thoughts, the hosts defended Gwyneth's comments, noting that she didn't "dis" either of her exes.

This led Jenna to ask Hoda if she "kisses and tells," to which the Today Show host said she feels it's a "betrayal" to your partner if you're in a relationship.

Hoda then fired the same question back at Jenna, who at first hesitated.

The TV star then revealed that she does not disclose details of her relationship, noting her 15-year marriage to her husband, Henry.

A photo of Jenna and Henry from their wedding night appeared on the screen, and the former first daughter seemed embarrassed over the snap.

She attempted to hide her discomfort by joking: "That was the night it all happened, y'all!"

The room erupted in laughter, and Hoda called her co-host a "bizarre human."
