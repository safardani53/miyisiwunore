BETTER late than never.

Calvin Harris has finally headed off on a honeymoon with Vick Hope four months after they tied the knot.

They have flown to Tulum, in Mexico, for a break to beat the January blues and couldn’t have looked happier as they splashed around in the sea.

The Radio 1 host looked sensational in a string bikini while Scottish DJ Calvin, who will turn 40 in a fortnight, showed off his chiselled body.

When he isn’t in sun-soaked luxury locations, Calvin has been releasing collaborations with Sam Smith and Eliza Rose.

Bizarre told last month how he also has a new single on the way with Rag’N’Bone Man, so it sounds like a seventh album can’t be far off.

Vick and Calvin tied the knot at Hulne Priory, Alnwick, Northumberland, on Saturday, September 9, last year. 

During the ceremony, the pair shared their personal vows and listened to poems. 

Nile Rodgers and Chic kicked off the reception with Luther Vandross’ 1981 hit Never Too Much, which was followed by songs by Madonna, Beyoncé, Pharrell Williams and David Bowie.

During the after party, guests were treated to an array of different dishes, including burgers, pizzas and Nigerian dishes, which was in a nod to Vick’s heritage.

They also had teepees and a firework display that lasted 10 minutes in the evening, lighting up the sky.

A source told The Sun: “Calvin and Vick’s wedding was a day none of their guests will ever forget.

“Their vows were really emotional and the marriage ceremony included poems like Ernest Hemmingway’s If There’s Empty Spaces In Your Heart and Lori Eberhai’s Devoted.

“They didn’t stop smiling all day. Calvin and Vick are so clearly madly in love, it was a truly beautiful day.”
