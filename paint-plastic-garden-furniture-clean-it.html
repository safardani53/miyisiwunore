Can you paint plastic garden furniture and how do you clean it?

IN this hot weather you might want to sit out in your garden and enjoy some time out in the sun.

If you have plastic garden furniture that has been stored away for a while though, it might need a quick run of paint and here we give you all the details you need to know about doing so.

Can you paint plastic garden furniture?

You CAN give your plastic garden furniture a new lease of life with paint.

There are specialist spray paints on the market.

Firstly clean your furniture with soapy water and make sure it's dry.

You can add an extra level by then spraying with a vinegar solution to ensure all traces of soap are gone.

You can also prepare by using a paint primer.

Before beginning to paint plastic furniture, make sure you have a spacious area where there is good ventilation, so that the paint also dries easily.

Apply the spray paint evenly - two coats are often required.

Let the paint dry and then inspect for any spots to cover-up.

Sanding afterwards can help the paint to integrate more into the furniture and also last longer.

So what you should do is grab some sandpaper or a sand sponge and gently brush it over the surface of your painted furniture, once the paint dries up.

How to clean plastic garden furniture?

Cleaning plastic garden furniture is very simple - you need some hot water, and then you can choose between laundry detergent, white vinegar or baking soda.

You can mix laundry detergent with some hot water and then just pour the mixture on the garden furniture.

Wipe the furniture with a scrub brush or with a microfibre cloth and then with a water hose, clean the mixture away.

You can also mix hot water with a quarter of a cup of white vinegar.

Splash it over the furniture and simply wipe it clean with a microfibre cloth.

Go over it once again with water to get the mixture off and just wipe it dry with a clean cloth.

Another option is baking soda, which you simply mix with water and apply it to the furniture the same as the others.

How to make pallet garden furniture?

If you're feeling in the mood for DIY, pallet garden furniture can be made to look elegant and style up your garden.

Although the wood is not as high quality, it is still very resistant to damage and that is why it will last for a long time.

To make pallet garden furniture, make sure that you have the following materials first:

  • Wooden pallets
  • Garden furniture chalk paint
  • Small roller and tray
  • Paint brush
  • Scatter cushions
  • Electric driller and screws

Now that you have the above tools, you can start building.

Get pallets

You can get some wooden pallets for free from supermarkets or construction sites.

Make sure to always ask the owner whether you can help yourself and also ensure that the pallets that you get are in good condition and without any cracks or splintering.

Sand the pallets

Go over the pallets with sandpaper or a sand sponge to soften it up a bit and remove any sharp edges and avoid splinters.

Also make sure that there aren't any nail coming out which can hurt you or others.

Paint the pallets

Using your painting materials, start going over the pallets to give them a nicer look.

Make sure that when you colour one side of the pallet, you leave it to dry first before colouring the other part.

Try not to paint on wet days or early in the morning because it'll make the paint harder to dry and also lose its gloss.

Create the base

Create the base of your furniture by stacking them one each other to a height that you're happy with.

Once you're satisfied, screw some nails in to secure the pallets together.

Attach the back

Now add a pallet to the back of the base in a vertical position, to create the back of your pallet chair.

Secure it in place with the screws and driller.
