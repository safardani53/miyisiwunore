Will Smith's $2.5m two-story motorhome has a sauna, cinema, private bar - and now you can stay for $

THE inside of actor Will Smith's multi-story camper has been revealed, and it's fully equipped with a sauna and movie theater.

The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air star's two-story motorhome is available to allow those interested a chance to spend a night like a movie star.

For just $295 a night, anyone can live like the fresh prince in his luxurious motorhome now located in Texas, The UK's Jam Press reported.

The A-list celeb has used the camper as his on-location trailer while filming movies in the past.

The award-winning actor reportedly used it while filming Men In Black 2.

The Heat trailer cost $2.5 million to make.

The camper comes with a sauna, cinema, and plenty of space to relax after a busy day on set.

The home-on-wheels was produced by Anderson Estates and Smith helped out with the design elements for his motor home.

With 1,200 square-feet of living space in total, the ground floor level boasts a fully functioning kitchen, full-size dining table, living area, makeup area, and meeting space.

The meeting room can also be used as a dressing room, additional sitting room, or even a bedroom.

There's a bar once you get to the second floor.

Upstairs, there's an entertaining room with a 50-inch TV and projector for a cinematic experience as well as a second bedroom.

Despite Smith no longer owning the trailer, the company said he has been a customer for over 15 years - using The Heat and other units.

The mobile home is currently based on a glamping resort in Austin, Texas.

"No expense was spared when this unit was built," The Anderson Estates listing stated.

"This unit still remains as one of the most advanced RVs in the world today."

The company has also provided luxe RVs for celebrities including Jamie Foxx, Mariah Carey, and Simon Cowell.
