The Unauthorized Saved By The Bell Story: Coming to Lifetime!

The Internet is so excited right now.

It’s so excited… it’s so excited… it’s so… SCARED.

What has users in such an uproar? The Unauthorized Saved by the Bell Story, of course, a television movie that will air September 1 on Lifetime.

Partly based on Dustin Diamond’s 2009 memoir, along with new interviews conducted for the film, the movie will tell the behind-the-scenes story of this former Saturday morning classic.

The stars have been cast by the original Saved by the Bell casting director (Robin Lippin), with the film delving into their (sexual!) relationships backstage, as well as they’re partying lifestyles at home.
