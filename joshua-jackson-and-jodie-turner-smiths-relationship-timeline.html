Joshua Jackson and Jodie Turner-Smith's Relationship Timeline

Joshua Jackson and Jodie Turner-Smith quickly fell head over heels for each other before their 2023 split.  

The duo sparked rumors of a romance after they were “all over each other” at Usher‘s 40th birthday party in October 2018. A source told Us Weekly at the time that the pair looked “super smitten” with one another and “danced together the whole night.” One month later, they were spotted holding hands on a casual coffee date in Los Angeles.

The couple quickly took their relationship to the next level after they were first rumored to be dating. In August 2019, the Daily Mail spotted the actors leaving a California courthouse with what appeared to be a marriage license. Just three months later, Turner-Smith sported a massive diamond ring on her left hand at the premiere of Queen & Slim in Los Angeles.

Shortly after the pair fueled marriage rumors, Us confirmed in December 2019 that the pair had secretly tied the knot and were expecting their first child together. In October 2023, Turner-Smith filed for divorce after four years of marriage.
