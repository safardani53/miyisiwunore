Snooki Calls Herself Hot Mess Following Son Angelos Birth

Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi is giving Jersey Shore fans an inside look at her new life as a mother of three, less than a week after the birth of her son Angelo. On Tuesday, June 4, the reality star shared a photo of herself in pajamas, describing herself as a “hot mess.”

“[Outfit of the day] by New Mom of 3,” Polizzi, 31, wrote in the caption of the Instagram post, adding “#hotmess” as a hashtag. So far, more than 90,000 Instagram users have “liked” the upload.

The Jersey Shore: Family Vacation star and her husband of four years, Jionni LaValle, welcomed Angelo to the family on Thursday, May 30, adding to a brood that already includes 6-year-old son Lorenzo and 4-year-old daughter Giovanna.

In her Instagram Stories, Polizzi shared videos of a sleepy, “milk drunk” Angelo and said that he looked like he could be Lorenzo’s “twin,” even down to his “little smirk.”

The MTV personality has been candid with Instagram followers about her childbirth process, even the less desirable aspects. “4 days postpartum & body is feeling good besides the fact that my cramps still feel like contractions!” she wrote in the caption to an Instagram selfie on Monday, June 3. “Still healing but can’t wait to jump back into the gym hardcore this summer! Time to be a fit mawma again for my nuggets.”

She later edited the caption to respond to users who trolled her for wanting to get back to her workout regimen. “Everyone is entitled to their own opinion, but don’t attack me for wanting to become strong and fit again after my pregnancy. My boobs are leaking and pulsating, my butthole is sore and my cramps are unbearable & I’m still wearing a diaper the size of Texas. Postpartum is a bitch. But I’m allowed to look on the bright side of things and want to get back into shape.”
