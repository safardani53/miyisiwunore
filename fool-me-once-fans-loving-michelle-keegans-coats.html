Fool Me Once fans are going mad for Michelle Keegans coats - where to buy her key looks including

Michelle Keegan’s latest role in Netflix drama Fool Me Once has gotten everyone talking.

But it seems chatter isn’t just about the plot. 

The 36-year-old plays ex-soldier Maya Stern who is trying to come to terms with the brutal murder of her husband. 

However, whilst fans are raving about the storyline there has also been a lot of interest in the characters outfits. 

One post about where Michelle’s black aviator jacket was from on the Facebook group ‘Family lockdown tips & ideas’ now has over 600 comments.

One user said: “I’m obsessed with the amount of coats she’s wearing!! I’m in episode 5 deep and she’s literally had a different coat every scene.”

Whilst another said: “Never noticed coats in a programme as much.” And “She has the best range of coats in this series.”

With many others agreeing on how much they love all the coats she is seen wearing.

After tracking down some of the coats worn by Michelle’s character in the series it seems she has quite the expensive taste, with brands such as Dolce and Gabbana, Burberry and Toteme all being worn. 

However luckily Fabulous’ fashion team have not only sought out where you can buy these coats from, but how you can get similar versions for just a fraction of the price. 

Most read in Fabulous

Aviator Jacket 

The coat that got fans of the show talking in the first place, Michelle’s aviator jacket was a hit with many wanting to know where it was from.

From designer Toteme the jacket will set you back £2,290.

So if that’s a bit out of your price range then fear not as you only have to head down the supermarket aisle to bag a dupe. 

F&F at Tesco currently has a great version of the coat for only £59.50, meaning you would save £2,230.50 - now that is a big saving indeed. 

Snap this coat up and you’ll have achieved one of the looks from the show for just a fraction of the price. 

  • Jacket, £2,290, Toteme
  • Dupe, £59.50, F&F at Tesco

Trench Coat

A classic coat option that really is timeless, in the show Michelle’s character Maya is seen multiple times out and about in her trench from Burberry.

Currently available to buy on Net-A-Porter it would set you back £1,890.

If you’re looking for something more affordable, Primark have a trench coat with a more purse friendly price tag at £35.

It has a tie detail and double breasted buttons, just like the Burberry version you will hardly be able to tell the difference - except from the £1,855 price tag difference.

  • Coat, £1,890, Burberry 
  • Dupe, £35, Primark 

Tweed Coat 

In the show we learn that Maya has married into a rich family and she really does have the wardrobe to go with. 

This longline tweed coat is from Dolce and Gabbana it is currently available to buy on the designers website for £2,550. 

Luckily Mango has a similar version which is currently on sale down from £169.99 to £79.99.

And whilst this may not be the coat you walk your dog in or use for the school run, it's a smart looking coat that will instantly elevate an outfit. 

Quiet luxury is still all the rage in 2024 and this coat is one that encapsulates this. 

Blue Blazer 

Everyone needs a blazer in their wardrobe, they can instantly dress up an outfit and can act as an extra layer. 

Michelle is seen here as Maya going back to her armed forces roots with a military style blazer. 

This blazer is from designer Veronica Beard and retails for £582, Karen Millen also has a similar version which is currently £135.20. 

With the same navy colour and gold button detailing it would be hard to tell which is which. 

Black Bomber 

This black bomber Michelle can be seen wearing here in the show is from French designer Maje. 

Stocked in Selfridges it would set you back £499 and is currently out of stock.

Asos however has a great dupe which is on sale for £24.75 down from £45. 

This would save you a mega £474.25 and is a perfect transeasonal jacket that will take you into the spring months of 2024.

Wear as Michelle’s character Maya has done with a colourful roll neck underneath for a pop of colour.

Cropped  Black Puffer

In Fool Me Once the puffer jacket worn by Michelle has been adapted for the show, with an embroidered logo on of her workplace. 

However, fans of the show were still loving the style of the coat and luckily there are some high street dupes around. 

This cropped puffer from Boohoo was originally £45 but has 44% off at the moment reducing the price to £25.
