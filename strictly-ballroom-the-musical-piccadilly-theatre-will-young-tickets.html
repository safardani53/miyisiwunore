When is Strictly Ballroom The Musical at Piccadilly Theatre? Tickets and cast with Will Young for Ba

STRICTLY Ballroom The Musical has opened in the West End - and it features an impressive cast.

But, how can you get tickets to the Baz Luhrmann stage show, and who's in the cast with Will Young? Let's get the lowdown...

When is Strictly Ballroom The Musical at Piccadilly Theatre and how do I get tickets?

Strictly Ballroom The Musical is previewing now at the Piccadilly Theatre.

Tickets for the previews are available NOW.

You can get them online, at the show's official website.

You can call the box office on 0844 871 7630.

You'll also be able to get them in person at the box office: Piccadilly Theatre 16 Denman Street London, W1D 7DY.

Who's in the cast?

Singer Will Young, who famously appeared in and later quit Strictly Come Dancing, is the biggest name in the show - he plays the character of Wally Strand.

What's Strictly Ballroom The Musical about?

The musical is based on Strictly Ballroom, the 1992 movie that was directed and co-written by Baz Luhrmann. It remains one of Australia's most successful films of all times.

It tells the story of an Australian ballroom dancer and his struggle to establish his personal style of dance on his way to win the Pan-Pacific Grand Prix Dancing Championship.

He performs unusual routine as he sets out to succeed with a new partner.

Luhrman has previously said Strictly Ballroom evolves around stories similar to David and Goliath, Cinderella and The Ugly Duckling.

What has Will Young said about Strictly Ballroom The Musical?

Will has said his role in the production is to be on the site of the audience.

He also said he jumped at the chance to appear in the stage show because he's a huge fan of Baz Luhrmann.

Will told the Metro: "I play the narrator so I’m on the side of the audience.

"I will hold your hand through this story. I talk to the audience and I can do anything. But at the end of the day, it’s all about the audience."
