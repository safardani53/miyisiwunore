Jessa Duggar reveals fifth baby's name in sweet new video just days after giving birth

JESSA Duggar has revealed the name of her fifth baby in a sweet new video.

The Counting On alum announced the moniker just days after giving birth.

Jessa, 31, shared a snapshot to Instagram from her hospital bed.

In the group photo, the former TLC star smiled as she carried her newborn in her hands.

Her fifth child appeared bundled in brownish sheets and had his head covered.

Sitting beside Jessa, her 1-year-old daughter Fern smiled as she looked on at her baby brother.

Husband Ben Seewald, 28, stood close to the hospital bed as he towered over them.

Jessa captioned her post: "Birth Story: Part 2 is now live on our YouTube channel!

"Kids meet [the] baby and find out [the] gender."

The TLC star closed: "We decide on a name and [bring] baby home!"

During the nearly 10-minute video, the couple gathered their four children: Fern, Ivy, 3, Spurgeon, 7; and Henry, 6.

She pointed the camera at the sleeping baby.

During her voiceover, the mom of five said: "We decided to settle on a name. And so, George Augustine, it is.

"I think it suits him well."

As the short video continued, Ben had a conversation with his mother, Guinn Seewald, and mentioned: "We were thinking about naming him George."

Guinn approved of the moniker because it reminded her of the legend of St. George — a soldier who defeated the dragon that had been extorting tribute from the villagers. 

'BABY IS HERE'

On Saturday, the 31-year-old star took to Instagram and announced the arrival of her baby boy, George.

Jessa cheered: "Baby is here and we couldn't be more grateful."

She also added a link to her YouTube account.

The arrival of Jessa's fifth child came after a bittersweet moment.

George was born almost exactly one year after his parents suffered a miscarriage.

The miscarriage occurred a few days before Christmas in 2022.

In early December, the TV personality opened up about her "missed miscarriage" and revealed how she did not experience any of the typical signs of the tragedy.

She discovered she had experienced the miscarriage about three weeks later.

Jessa shared: "I just immediately started crying and I was so grateful in that moment that I hadn't gone to the appointment by myself, and Ben was there, and he put his arms around me.

"It was crazy because my body didn't acknowledge that."

She concluded: "I didn't have signs or symptoms that anything was wrong."

'OUR RAINBOW BABY'

In September, Jessa and Ben released a YouTube video announcing her pregnancy.

She said: "After a heartbreaking loss of our baby last year, just this past week we found out some wonderful news."

The star then panned the camera and showed off a positive pregnancy test.
