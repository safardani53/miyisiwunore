Kanye West Tells Fans To Boycott Louis Vuitton After VP Denies To Meet Him Hollywood Life

Kanye West’s Louis Vuitton Boycott:

Kanye told the station: “What happened was while I was out in Paris. I wanted to meet with the head of Louis Vuitton, and he said I don’t understand why we need to meet with you. Let me explain to you why you need to meet with me. Everybody in New York City right now don’t buy any Louis Vuitton until after January. Now do you want to meet with me? Now do you want to meet with me? Influence. They think that I don’t realize my power.”

A day after his first outburst against Louis Vuitton, he appeared on yet another NYC radio station, Power 105.1’s The Breakfast Club, on Nov. 26 where he took his frustrations out on the fashion world. “These people do not take me seriously. Everybody at the Louis Vuitton store in Atlanta don’t buy anything until after January. Thats how much power I have,” he said.
