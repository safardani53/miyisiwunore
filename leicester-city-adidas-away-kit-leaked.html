Leicester City's new adidas away kit leaked with fans unsure what to think

LEICESTER CITY'S away kit for the 2018-19 season from new manufacturers adidas may have been leaked and Foxes fans are unsure what to think.

After six years with Puma, Leicester announced a kit partnership with adidas two weeks ago but are yet to release any further news or images on kits for next season.

But one rumoured kit, a light and dark grey number with orange trim based on adidas' Condivo 18 template, has been doing the rounds on social media this afternoon.

The kit was posted on Twitter at lunchtime by Footy Headlines and the verdict is far from unanimous from Foxes supporters.
