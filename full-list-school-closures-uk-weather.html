Full list of school closures as UK hit by ice warning and temperatures plunge to -5C - see if you're

DOZENS of schools have been forced to close their gates after temperatures plunged to -5 overnight.

The Met Office has ice and snow warnings in place in parts of the country, with Brits waking up this morning to the mercury below zero.

Forecasters say the white stuff could fall along the east coast of the country, with a warning in place from Scotland to Chelmsford - urging locals to take care until 11am today

There are also ice warnings in place for parts of Northern Ireland, and around Truro, Plymouth and through Exeter until 10am.

And the wintery conditions have seen some schools forced to tell students to stay home.

Here is a list of schools that we understand are closed for the day - or opening late. If your child's isn't on the list and you have doubts, contact their school.
