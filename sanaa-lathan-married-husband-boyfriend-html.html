Why Actress Sanaa Lathan Resorts To Boyfriend &amp; Dating Instead Getting Married

Super talented American actress Sanaa Lathan has millions of admirers all over the world. Very active with her dating life, all her fans are wondering when the 46-year-old beautiful actress gets married, but she claims she is in no rush.

Sanaa Lathan, 46, is a renowned American actress and voice actress. She is well recognized for her roles in box-office hits like The Best Man, The Best Man Holiday, Love & Basketball, Now You See Me 2 among others.

Lathan worked in theaters too and was nominated for a Tony Award, for her amazing performance on Broadway, an American theater in A Raisin in the Sun.

Sanaa Lathan’s Bio: Childhood, Parents & Siblings

Sanaa was born on September 19, 1971, in Beverly Hills, New York City. Her mom is Eleanor McCoy, an actress, and dancer whereas her father is Stan Lathan, a film director. So we can say Sanaa was born in the midst of Hollywood glitz.

Sanaa completed her graduation in English from University of California, Berkeley. After that, she earned her master's degree in drama from Yale University, New Haven.

The actress has a brother named Tendaji Lathan, who works as a DJ. She also has three sisters; Lilliane Lathan, a professional photographer, Ariel Lathan, and Colette Lathan, the youngest one, who are all as gorgeous as her. 

Sanaa Lathan’s Career In Brief: Love & Basketball, Now You See Me 2, Net Worth In 2018

The New York native began her early acting career in the late 90s.

She first got the taste of acting from her role in Blade in 1988. But she caught the limelight when she played “Robyn” in 1999 hit The Best Man which also landed her an NAACP(National Association for the Advancement of Colored People) Image Award nomination.

The actress then went on to win the BET Award, the Black Reel Award and the NAACP Image Award for her scintillating performance in 2000’s hit film Love & Basketball for her role as Monica Wright.

After this her career caught pace and she went on to star in many Hollywood hits like Alien vs. Predator (2004), Contagion (2011), Now You See Me 2 (2016), American Assassin (2017) being only a few of them.

With that, she has been able to maintain a strong net worth of $4 million.

Sanaa Lathan's Views On Being A Black Woman In Hollywood 

Carrying 20 years of experience in working in the movie industry, Sana reflects a lot more on her evolution as well as Hollywood's.

During the honor of the launch of Ralph Lauren Fragrances' new 'Woman' perfume, Sanna shared her perspective on what it meant to be a woman in the industry, especially what it meant to be a black woman in Hollywood.

I always talk about the fairy tales that we grew up on - Cinderella, Rapunzel, Sleeping Beauty, Snow White - I mean, these are the stories that we are read as little girls to show us what is valued and what is beautiful and what we should want and (black women are) not reflected in that. (-said by Sanaa Lathan to E! News | 30 October 2018)

Sanaa further expressed her views on how the movies and TV shows are showing people as they really are and she was more than proud of the change that has arrived.

Sanaa's Rumored Dating Life With Boyfriends, French Montana & Regina Hall, Shares Children With Them?

Though the American Assassin star has been linked with a few Hollywood hotties like Tyrese Gibson, Oscar-nominated singer, Omar Epps, her Love and Basketball co-star, among few, she has never been married, nor she has any children.

Sanna, in 2015, was rumored to be dating French Montana, a famous rapper. Amid speculations about them, stories emerged alleging Sanaa was carrying French’s child. Both Sanaa and Montana later denied the rumors. 

The following year, in 2016, another rumor relating to Sanaa and Regina Hall, American actress and comedian began to circulate.

In the end, these proved to be just rumors when Sanaa spoke in an interview with Essence (2016), about their friendship, bonding and denied they were dating.
