Who is Emma in Taylor Swift's new song?

After Taylor Swift shared the track list for Speak Now (Taylor's Version) on Monday, June 5, 2023, fans began pondering the identity of Emma from the album's eighteenth track, When Emma Falls in Love.

The track is among six previously unreleased "From The Vault" songs that fans got to hear for the first time on July 7.

However, following the track's release, evidence largely points to the Stone theory.

As per Entertainment Weekly, many of When Emma Falls in Love's lyrics suggest that the Stone rumors are true.

During the song's first verse, Swift sings: "When Emma falls in love, she calls up her mom, jokes about the ways that this one could go wrong. She waits and takes her time, 'cause little Miss Sunshine always thinks it's gonna rain."

It would make sense for Stone to discuss her dating life with her mom, as the outlet reports that the pair share a close relationship.

It also notes that "little Miss Sunshine" could be referring to when Stone sang along to Natasha Bedingfield's song, Pocketful of Sunshine, in the movie Easy A.

Swift later sings: "She's so New York when she's in L.A."

Entertainment Weekly notes that this line could refer to some of the time the megastars spent together. It reports that they previously were spotted hanging out in New York on more than one occasion, although Stone was living in Los Angeles at the time.
