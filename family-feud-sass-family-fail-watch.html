‘Family Feud’: WATCH The Biggest Fail Ever

Family Feud brings families together that bring their A-game — well, Anna Sass brought her F-game to set on Tuesday, May 6, when the Sass family played the game.

During the “Fast Money” round, which is the portion of the show that picks two family members to play rapid fire and accumulate 200 points together, Anna just couldn’t get it together.

After her father completely killed it, scoring 182 points, she only had 18 points to go to win her family $20,000. But, she gained a big fat zero.

Just take a look at her family’s reaction. Even Steve Harvey is in awe of Anna!

We get it. Playing for money on national TV is a lot of pressure, and she had to follow up her dad’s round, which was tough. But we were cringing when Anna answered “a married couple may be deeply in ____” with “deeply in marriage.” Oh, Anna.
