Khloe Kardashian throws shade at sister Kourtney with embarrassing post amid nasty family feud

KHLOE Kardashian has thrown some serious shade at her older sister Kourtney, amid their brewing personal battle.

The long rumored rift between the famous sisters was finally revealed in a trailer for Season 3 of The Kardashians.

Khloe, 38, took to her snapchat to share a meme of a throwback photo of Kourtney, 44, from 2017.

In the historical snap, Kourtney was headed to lunch with her sister Kim, when she apparently decided to take a phone call mid-stride.

The reality star was dressed in a loose, mustard-colored, cropped sweatshirt with destroyed denim as she seemed to chat.

But when a fan zoomed in on the phone, the app screen was visible - something that usually isn't when a call is in progress.

The discovery was instantly turned into a variety of sarcastic memes, declaring: "When you see someone you don't want to talk to so you pretend you're on the phone." or "When you see you're ex in public, but you're all alone."

Although the meme seemed to be buried and forgotten about, Khloe brought it back on Tuesday with her own snarky spin.

"Whenever I'm in an uncomfortable situation:" she shared above reposts of the pic.

SISTER VS. SISTER

Khloe and Kourtney have found themselves in their own uncomfortable situation lately, especially after their ongoing feud, including vitriol towards Kim, was exposed in the trailer for the new season of The Kardashians.

Earlier this week, a bombshell trailer for The Kardashians season three was released.

In April 2022, Kourtney and her husband Travis Barker, 47, held their wedding in Portofino, Italy.

In new footage from the event, the mom of three angrily accused Kim, 42, of using her wedding as a "business opportunity."

Kim asked sibling Kendall Jenner, 27: "She's mad at me?"

In a confessional, Khloe claimed Kourtney was "livid."

While speaking to her other sister Kylie Jenner, 25, Kendall added: "She felt like her wedding vibes were, like, stripped from her."

THE DEFENSE RESTS

Kim though defended herself: "I'm really confused about how this narrative came into her head.

"I couldn't have been more mindful."

The TV star continued explaining: "I said: 'Don't do anything that Kourtney wore to her wedding."

Kourtney's sister was referring to her collaboration with Dolce & Gabbana. who famously sponsored Kourtney and Travis' wedding. 

Months later, Kim worked with the Italian fashion house to create her own collection.

After hearing Kim's explanation, Kendall reluctantly admitted: "I see both sides," as Kylie agreed.
