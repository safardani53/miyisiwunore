Farrah Abraham's Dad: Her Porn Video was NOT My Idea!

OMG so awkward.

This was a topic even Dr. Drew didn’t want to touch (wrong choice of words) during part one of Teen Mom OG reunion.

"I’m gonna ask a tough question, dad-to-dad.  The elephant in the room is some of the exploitation that she’s been through," Dr. Drew said, pointing to Farrah Abraham.

"I feel for you," Dr. Drew told Michael Abraham.  "I can imagine how conflicted you must have been about that.  I don’t even know how to talk about it, frankly."

Abraham nodded silently, then revealed that "when that video was announced, it was portrayed that I negotiated her deal."

"So it’s as though you served her up," Dr. Drew added.

Abraham agreed and continued.

"When you get involved with the wrong management company – I’ll just say – they ask you, they tell you to do certain things. In retrospect, I would have never gone and done that."

Dr. Drew saw a red flag.

"You trusted some people who weren’t worthy of your trust at the time," he said to Michael.

"Right"

"Is that a pattern for you, in your life?" he asked

"No," Michael said.

"It is for Farrah," Dr. Drew pointed out

"It has been, but I’ve seen Farrah breaking out of it," Michael explained.

"I see it too," Dr. Drew said, "but I’m wondering how you got sucked into it."

EEEK.

"That particular thing was because I wanted to be by her side, because six days before there was a release that my daughter had an adult video," Michael admitted.

"How did you get through all that," Dr. Drew asked, as Abraham started to choke up.

"A lot of hope.  A lot of prayer," Michael answered, holding back tears.

In another sneak peak, Farrah admitted to Dr. Drew that she suffered a "horrific" childhood incident, which was not specified in the clip.  

The MTV star, who battled with her producer this past season, got emotional as she and Dr. Drew spoke more about her past.

"It’s so sad," she told Dr. Drew when he noticed her welling up.

Part of of Teen Mom OG reunion airs tonight at 10/9 c on MTV.
