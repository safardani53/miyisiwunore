Briana Dejesus News, Photos and Videos Hollywood Life

Bio:
Briana DeJesus (born Briana DeJesus on May 21, 1994 in Orlando, Florida) is an American reality TV star. She was featured on season four of the MTV reality series 16 & Pregnant, and was one of the four girls featured on the spin-off MTV series Teen Mom 3. She joined the cast of Teen Mom 2 in 2017. Briana lives in Brooklyn, New York and gave birth to her first daughter Nova Star DeJesus when she was 17 years old. Her sister, Brittany DeJesus, was also pregnant at the same time as Briana, but opted to get an abortion. Both women’s decisions were featured on her episode of 16 & Pregnant. Briana has been raising her daughter as a single mom with the help of her family after her baby daddy Devoin Austin II dropped out of her life. Briana graduated high school a year ahead of her class and took classes at a community college. She also worked part-time at a Polo retail store.

Best Known For:
Briana DeJesus is best known for appearing on the MTV reality series 16 & Pregnant, and now the new MTV series Teen Mom 3.

Personal Life:
Briana DeJesus found herself pregnant at the age of 16 by her boyfriend, Devoin Austin II. After her daughter Nova Star DeJesus was born on September 9, 2011, her boyfriend broke up with her. She In June 2017, she gave birth to Stella Star with Luis Hernandez. Briana has raised both of her daughters as a single mom, but dated Teen Mom 2 co-star Javi Marroquin in 2017. Although they hinted at marriage, they broke up in January 2018, which Briana attributed her decisions to have full-body plastic surgery treatments and not to move into his home.
