Vivienne Westwood Biography

Vivienne Westwood is the famous fashion designer from the United Kingdom and she is the one who introduced punk and new wave fashions in conventional fashion. She has also established herself as a businesswoman.

Vivienne Westwood was born on April 8, 1941, in a middle-class family as Vivienne Isabel Swire, in Tintwistle, Derbyshire, England of United Kingdom, to Gordon Swire and Dora Swire. She is English and is of White Ethnicity.

Marriage, Husband, and Children

In 1962, Vivienne Westwood was married to Derek Westwood. She also has a child with him. She got divorced with him in 1965.

After that she began her romantic love affairs with Malcolm McLaren. He is the famous musician and also the clothes designer and boutique owner. From these love affairs, she gave birth to Joseph Corré.

In 1992, she got married to Andreas Kronthaler. He is the also the fashion designer and he was once her fashion student. From that time on she is living as her loving spouse.  

Career & Net Worth

From her early career, she was interested in punk. She also designed several clothes being inspired by prostitutes, bikers, and fetishists. After she began her punk era she started her own fashion collection.

How much is her net worth?

From her fashion designing career, she earned a massive amount of salary and her net worth is rumored to be around $185 million, as of 2019.
