Punky Penguin ice creams are heading to UK stores... and we feel like kids again

AS well as sun and sand, childhood holidays meant endless amounts of ice cream and ice lollies.

Whether it’s Fab lollies, Screwballs or Funny Feet you fondly remember, the humble ices have the power take you back to the heady days of summers past.

But for those who spent their breaks abroad in the 1990s,  the Punky Penguin is the top of the nostalgia list.

So here’s the good news - the penguin-shaped plastic pot full of vanilla ice cream could soon be hitting shops in the UK.

UK wholesaler Holdsworth foods is now selling them in bulk at 12 for £20.40, says Pretty52, which works out at £1.70 per bird.

Sadly, you can only buy them if you are in the trade but the fact that they are available means these cute little penguins should by waddling into corner shops and supermarkets any day now.

We’ll be waiting.
