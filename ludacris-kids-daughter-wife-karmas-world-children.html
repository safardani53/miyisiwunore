How many kids does Ludacris have?

HITMAKER and movie star Ludacris juggles responsibilities between his high-profile career and fatherhood.

While Ludacris is not on movie sets or in recording studios, fans attempt to keep up with his life off of the stage - which is centered around his family.

How many kids does Ludacris have?

Ludacris - real name Christopher Brian Bridges - is the father of four daughters.

His children include:

  • Karma Bridges (August 2001)
  • Cai Bella Bridges (December 9, 2013)
  • Cadence Gaelle Bridges (June 4, 2015)
  • Chance Oyali Bridges (July 28, 2021)

His two youngest children, Cadence and Chance, are from his current wife, Eudoxie Mbouguiengue, while his two older daughters are from previous relationships.

Karma's mother is Christine White, an Atlanta-based attorney, while Cai Bella's mother is Tamika Fuller.

When does Ludacris' show Karma's World premiere?

Inspired by fatherhood, Ludacris and Netflix teamed up for the release of a childrens' animation show named after his second daughter titled Karma's World.

The first season of Karma's World premiered on Netflix on October 15, 2021.

Season two of the series was distributed to the platform on March 10, 2022.

The second edition of the program tackled social issues such as healthy image and body positivity.

Ludacris spoke of the parallels between the show and what he witnessed with Karma and his other daughters.

The rapper told The Today Show: "There was a stage where she was gaining a little weight and she was a little self-conscious about her weight."

"I always just constantly reminded her how beautiful she is and, you know, understanding that exercise is important, how you eat, all these different things."

The actor added: "But as long as you love your kids, and you teach them that everybody has different body types and body sizes, just like this episode that we have in season two, that's coming out Thursday, that's what's most important - is for children to love themselves and for them to make the choices that they want to make."

"But as long as they feel loved, I feel like everything is going to be perfectly fine and OK because everyone is different and we have to celebrate each other's similarities and differences."

Who is Ludacris' wife Eudoxie Mbouguiengue?

Since December 26, 2014, Ludacris has been married to Gabonese model - Eudoxie Mbouguiengue.

Along with modeling, Mbouguiengue is also an entrepreneur who founded Untouched Angels charity and is the author of the 2016 book, Unspoken Angel: My Story Through Her Eyes.

The couple met on Ludaday, which is a weekend tradition intended to bring families and celebrities together, and while Ludacris admitted to infidelity, they have been able to fix their relationship and continue to grow their family.

Ludacris popped the question in a romantic proposal aboard a private plane.

"She didn't say yes. She said HELL YES! #milehighproposal," he said in an Instagram post following the engagement.

Some of his recognizable songs include Rollout (My Business), Move B***h, Stand Up, Money Maker, and My Chick Bad.
