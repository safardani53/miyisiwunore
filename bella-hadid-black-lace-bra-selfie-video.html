Bella Hadid Wears Black Lace Bra In Selfie Video Hollywood Life

Bella Hadid, 24, showed The Weeknd, 30, what he’s missing! The daughter of Yolanda Hadid finished her beauty look with a deep brown lipstick, adding to the ’90s vibe of her sexy ensemble. The star appeared to be getting glammed up with makeup artist Sam Visser and hair stylist Jennifer Yepez ahead of an appearance at the Michael Kors store inside Macy’s.

Of note, the Toronto native and Bella are both no longer following each other on social media.

The duo appeared to play lovers in the sexy new, with many fans speculating about a potential off-screen romance!

Bella and The Weekend began their on-again, off-again romance back in 2015, last splitting in Aug. 2019. Fresh off their break-up, they awkwardly ran into each other at Catch One nightclub, prompting Bella to leave after the Canadian-born singer showed up. The Weeknd and Bella appear to be on better terms recently, after bumping into each other in August at rehearsals for the MTV Video Music Awards (he was performing, while she was presenting Lady Gaga with the MTV Tricon Award).
