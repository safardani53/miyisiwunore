Lizzy Caplan, born June 30, 1982, is a talented actress, known for her snarky style and off-beat characters. Her first gig was on ‘Freaks And Geeks’ (199-2000), and she had a huge role in ‘Mean Girls’ (2004), and ‘Cloverfield’ (2008). She also starred on the television shows ‘Related’ (2005–2006), ‘The Class’ (2006–2007), and ‘Party Down’ (2009–2010).

Her next big TV role was on the Showtime series ‘Masters Of Sex’ from 2013 to 2016. She received nominations for a Primetime Emmy, two Satellite Awards, and a Critics’ Choice Award for the role. In 2019 she was in the Hulu anthology series ‘Castle Rock’. Her other film appearances include ‘Hot Tub Time Machine’, ‘127 Hours (both 2010), Save the Date, Bachelorette (both 2012), The Interview (2014), Now You See Me 2, Allied (both 2016), and Extinction’ (2018).

Through it all, Lizzie has stayed so fashionable. Here she stuns in white at the 8th Annual ‘Reel Stories, Real Lives’ Event Benefiting MPTF in LA on Nov. 4, 2019. The long scoop neck dress was effortlessly elegant and she wore her brunette locks short.
