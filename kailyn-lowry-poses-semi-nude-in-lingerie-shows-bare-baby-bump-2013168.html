Kailyn Lowry Pregnancy Photo Shoot: Teen Mom 2 Star Poses Semi-Nude

Dare to bare! Teen Mom 2 star Kailyn Lowry decided to capture her second pregnancy on camera in a very special way — by posing semi-nude. Expecting her second child, Lowry recently put her bare baby bump on display for a sexy maternity photo shoot.

According to Radar Online, the 21-year-old MTV star had photographer Katie Hendrick capture the maternity photos two weeks ago in Pennsylvania. In one full-body shot, Lowry poses in a lacy black lingerie top and matching underwear while showing off her pregnant belly. In another sweet photo, the second-time mom-to-be sits cross-legged wearing an open blouse that exposes her bare bump.

Multiple sources confirmed to Us Weekly in March that Lowry and husband Javi Marroquin were expecting their first child together. (Lowry is already mom to son Isaac, 3, with ex-boyfriend Jo Rivera.)

Lowry tied the knot with the U.S. airman back in September after a year of dating. The two are currently planning a larger wedding celebration.
