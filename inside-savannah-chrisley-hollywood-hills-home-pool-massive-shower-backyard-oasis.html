Inside Savannah Chrisley and Chases swanky Hollywood Hills home featuring a pool, massive shower

SAVANNAH Chrisley and her older brother Chase left the family home in Nashville for a swanky Hollywood Hills pad in 2019, and have yet to look back.

The brother and sister duo live together as they make their way in the city of Angels, and their Los Angeles home is the perfect base, with floor to ceiling windows on the ground floor letting in natural light, and a pool and jacuzzi in the backyard with stunning city views.

The first floor of their home is open-plan with an all-white kitchen and farmhouse details including rustic kitchen bar stools and an eight-person dining table.

The living area has a cozy sofa with comfy cushions and flat-screen TV, while upstairs Chase was stuck with the smaller bedroom while Savannah managed to snag the master bedroom with a huge en-suite - that may be bigger than Chase's bedroom - featuring a standalone tub and walk-in shower.

The master also features a balcony but it's the garden when the magic happens.

A decent-sized pool features pool floats including a flamingo and pizza slices, perfect for having friends over for summer soirees.

There are also sun loungers and chairs, as well as an air hockey table.

Savannah and Chase found fame on the USA Network show Chrisley Knows Best and also featured in their own series, Growing Up Chrisley which saw them move to LA.

Savannah, 22, was engaged to be married to hockey player Nic Kerdiles after 18 months of dating but Thursday's season 8 premiere episode of Chrisley Knows Best documented Savannah and Nic informing her parents that she had called off the wedding.
