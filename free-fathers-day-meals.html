Where Dads Can Eat For Free

Here's where you can find free food deals and specials for Father's Day 2022. Because why not save money while celebrating the dads in your life?

Consider this your annual reminder to phone your old man on Father's Day this Sunday. And although those who live far away from their fathers might have to do with a quick chat, those who live closer to their fathers can take them out for great meals without breaking the bank.

Article continues below advertisement

This year fathers eat for free at certain restaurants, and there are many other chains offering enticing specials that will be sure to provide your dad with two of his favorite things: free food and quality time with you. 

Keep reading for a list of restaurants offering free Father's Day meals on June 19.

Article continues below advertisement

BJ's Restaurant and Brewhouse

If your dad likes brews or a wide variety of entrees, BJ's is for you! Dads dining in-restaurant on June 18 and 19 will receive a free commemorative Father’s Day glass and a 20% off VIP card that they can use from June 20 through July 10.

Article continues below advertisement

California Pizza Kitchen

CPK has a deal that's sure to make your dad happy. Buy one pizza, pasta or salad for dine-in or takeout on Father's Day and get one free on your next visit! Offer available on June 18 and 19.

Article continues below advertisement

Fazoli's

Fazoli's has the perfect way to celebrate Father's Day with free wings! From June 15 through June 19, enter the code FATHER in the promo section of the Fazoli’s app to unlock an offer for a free order of five boneless wings with any $10 purchase or more.

Article continues below advertisement

McDonald's

Through June 30, you can get a free Happy meal with any GrubHub order of $15 or more.

Moe's Southwest Grill

Every Sunday, including this Father's Day, kids under 12 get a free meal with an adult meal purchase of $6 or more.

Article continues below advertisement

PDQ

This Father's Day, PDQ is offering a free sub (sandwich only) with any meal or salad purchase on June 19. This offer is available in-store only, while supplies last, so make sure you stop by!

Pollo Campero

Pollo Campero is offering $5 off any purchase of $25 with code BESTDAD. This deal runs from June 17 through June 19.

Article continues below advertisement

Rosa Mexicano

All Rosa Mexicano locations will be making dad's entree complimentary with other entree purchases. This deal is only available on June 19 for in-restaurant dining, so make sure you take advantage while you can! It also excludes ribeye and parrilladas entrees.

Wendy's

Happy Father's Day, and happy eating!
