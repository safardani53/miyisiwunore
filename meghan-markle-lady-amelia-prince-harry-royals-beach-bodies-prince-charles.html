After Prince Charles rocked floral trunks, here's the rest of the royal clan showing off their beach

PRINCE Charles caused a stir yesterday as he was snapped in floral swimming trunks on the beach in Barbados.

The heir to the throne looked buff at 70 as he frolicked among the waves – but he’s not the only royal family member to have stripped off to reveal their beach body.

Here, Fabulous Digital has rounded up some sun-soaked snaps of the famous family kicking back and relaxing on their holidays...

Prince Harry

Dashing Prince Harry raised temperatures when he was pictured soaking up some rays in the Caribbean, and it wasn’t due to his flaming red hair.

Sporting some red swimming shorts and sunglasses, the dashing prince looked relaxed in 2007 as he spent time in Barbados, like his dad.

Unlike Prince Charles, Prince Harry reportedly rode the Caribbean waves on a wakeboard, showing off the lean Windsor genes.

He is now happily married to Meghan Markle, but this trip was spent with young sweetheart Chelsy Davy, who was captured watching her boyfriend playing on the surf.

Meghan Markle

Being a lover of yoga and regular fitness, it’s no surprise that Prince Harry’s new wife Meghan Markle looks sensational in swimwear.

When she married film producer Trevor Engelson in 2011, she was snapped in a yellow bikini during the sun-drenched celebrations in Jamaica.

She also posted an eye-catching photo doing an impressive yoga move in a two-piece swimsuit on her now-deleted personal Instagram account.

Looks like we’ll be booking into a yoga-class asap!

Prince William

He may be the heir to the throne, but Prince William knows how to unwind in the sun.

The future king was spotted enjoying some rest and relaxation on a swanky yacht near Ibiza, with his then-girlfriend Kate Middleton in 2006.

Looking like he’d caught the sun, the royal was pictured having some down-time on the top deck wearing some fetching red swimming shorts.

Kate Middleton

Kate Middleton caught Prince William’s eye as she strutted her stuff in a see-through outfit on a fashion catwalk at the University of St Andrews, when they were both students.

And she looked every inch the movie star as she lounged in a white Bond girl-esque bikini on the luxury yacht with her partner in Spain during their break.

Showing off washboard abs and lean legs, the tanned royal-to-be certainly showed why she stood out for Prince William.

Pippa Middleton

Kate’s sister Pippa also clearly inherited the sporty Middleton genes, and can certainly rock a bikini on the beach like no other.

The Duchess of Cambridge's sister welcomed first child Arthur in October last year, but was pictured looked lean in a white bikini in January, just weeks after the birth.

It’s no wonder that Pippa looks trim, as the fitness fanatic regularly works out doing sports from boxing and indoor climbing to jumping on a trampoline.

Princess Diana

Princess Diana was a firm fan of spending quality time with her boys, and took them on a number of family holidays to far-flung beach resorts.

The royal was snapped looking care-free on a break to the Caribbean in 1993, wearing a red bikini and sunglasses in the sea.

In another shot during earlier years of her marriage to Prince Charles, she was spotted posing on a yacht in Majorca wearing an eye-catching strapless floral two-piece.

Lady Amelia Windsor

Last year, Lady Amelia Windsor threw the royal rulebook out the window and caused a furore after going topless on the beach in Ibiza.

The 23-year-old model, who is 38th in line to the throne, strolled around Santa Eulalia wearing just pink bikini bottoms.

She also looked happy and relaxed as she read a book and cooled off with a dip in the sea.

The Duke of Kent’s Granddaughter, who has been named the most beautiful royal by Tatler, was on holiday with her sister Marina.

The Queen

You’ll be hard-pressed to find snaps of Her Majesty stretched out on a beach in a swimsuit.

But the monarch was pictured in 1953 having a swimming lesson when she was about 27-years-old.

Donning a fetching swimming cap instead of her usual colourful trademark hats, the young royal splashed around in the water with sister Princess Margaret.

Prince Philip

Back in the day, Prince Phillip is known to have had a reputation for being a bit of a dish.

And a black-and-white image shows off his chiselled torso in Turkey in 1951.

The royal would have been around 30-years-old at the time and the snap was captured when he was with the royal navy aboard the HMS Magpie.

Like his grandson Prince Harry, Prince Philip was a fan of water sports, and is said to have done water skiing during the trip.

Princess Margaret

The Queen’s sister was captured taking a dip in a lake in Windsor at Sunninghill Park in Windsor.

The royal rebel looked incredible in a fetching one-piece and swimming hat, as she made the most of her UK summer in 1964.

Princess Beatrice

Princess Beatrice and her sister Eugenie have come under fire in the past for their jet-setting lifestyles, despite not being working royals.

One of their many lavish getaways included a yacht break in St Tropez, where Beatrice was snapped in a polka dot halter-neck bikini.

Making the most of her down-time the royal smiled in the sun with pals.
