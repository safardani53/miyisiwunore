Chic, Sans Underwear in UK InStyle

Selena Gomez dons several chic outfits in a new photo shoot for InStyle UK, which features her on the cover of the magazine’s January 2016 issue.

In it, she appears to have eschewed the use of a bra.

The 23-year-old Gomez has showcased revealing looks in the past, including on her new Revival album cover, in which she appears topless.

She has also been the target of body shaming trolls online.

A frequent subject of tabloid and social media gossip, Gomez has not hesitated to fire back at those critics when she feels they cross a line.

"Other days I’m like, "I’m not even going to bother.’"
