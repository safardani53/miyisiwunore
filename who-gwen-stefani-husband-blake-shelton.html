Who is Gwen Stefani's husband, Blake Shelton?

BLAKE Shelton and Gwen Stefani met while working as judges on the hit TV show, The Voice.

The country superstar and the pop icon have had highly successful careers in the entertainment industry.

Who is Blake Shelton?

Blake Shelton was born on June 18, 1976, in Ada, Oklahoma.

He is a country music singer-songwriter and a former judge on the popular television series, The Voice.

He made his music debut in 2001 with his lead single, Austin, from a self-titled album.

The track spent five weeks at Number One on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart.

The star singer has accumulated several accolades for his 12 albums.

Shelton served as a judge on The Voice since its first season, which aired on April 26, 2011.

On October 11, 2022, he announced via Instagram that season 23 would be his last.

Season 23 premiered on March 6, 2023, on NBC, and concluded on May 23, 2023.

Shelton has been married three times.

He was married to his high school sweetheart, Kaynette Williams, from 2003 to 2006.

His union with country artist Miranda Lambert lasted from 2011 to 2015.

Shelton began dating musician Gwen Stefani in 2015 after meeting on The Voice while they were both judges.

He proposed to Stefani on October 27, 2021.

Stefani announced their engagement as she flashed her beautiful diamond ring on social media while sharing a kiss with the country star.

She added the caption: "Yes, please!"

Shelton shared the same picture on his social media accounts and wrote: "Hey Gwen, thanks for saving my 2020... And the rest of my life.. I love you. I heard a YES!"

On July 3, 2021, the couple tied the knot in an intimate ceremony at Shelton's Oklahoma ranch.

Sources previously reported that they built a chapel at his home with the intention of marrying there.

How many kids does Blake Shelton have?

Shelton has no children of his own.

However, Stefani has three kids with her ex-husband, rock star Gavin Rossdale.

Stefani and Rossdale tied the knot in 2002. Their divorce was finalized in 2016.

Stefani's three sons include Kingston (born May 26, 2006), Zuma (born August 21, 2008), and Apollo (born February 28, 2014).

Shelton gets along with his wife's kids, according to In Touch Weekly. 

The outlet reported that Shelton said in a March 2018 interview with Today: "Having them around  — I don’t even know how to describe it. It’s so much fun.”

Rumors previously circulated that the couple want to start a family of their own and that they may be looking for a surrogate to make that happen, according to iHeartRadio.
