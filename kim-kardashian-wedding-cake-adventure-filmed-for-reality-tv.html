Kim Kardashian: Wedding Cake Adventure Filmed For Reality TV

Kim Kardashian and her NBA fiance Kris Humphries joined other family members as they engaged in the time-honored tradition of picking out a wedding cake.

Actually most couples don’t bring along the whole fandamily but when you are looking for good footage for your reality show Keeping Up with the Kardashians, everyone gets into the act.

The crew and cast of KUWTK hit Hansen’s Cakes in Beverly Hills for some taste-testing. The group included matriarch Kris Jenner, sister Khloe and brother Rob. They all took a turn at the obligatory forkful of cake as the cameras captured the family bliss.

The black tie wedding is slated for August 20, just up the road from Oprah‘s mega-mansion in picturesque Montecito.
