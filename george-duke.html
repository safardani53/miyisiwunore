Who is George Duke:

George Duke is a famous Composer. He was born on January 12, 1946 and his birthplace is San Rafael, CA. George is also well known as, Jazz fusion pioneer who wowed audiences with ‘The Jean-Luc Ponty Experience with the George Duke Trio,’ an early fusion jazz album. He and Frank Zappa were longtime collaborators.

George is originated from United States. He provided original compositions for Michael Jackson’s “Off the Wall” album.
