Country Singer David Morris Marries Wife Miranda Harrison

After ten years of dating, country music singer David Morris and his girlfriend Miranda Harrison got married in a dreamy, vintage-inspired wedding ceremony.

The couple exchanged vows on October 7, 2023, at the Madonna Inn in San Luis Obispo, California. The Madonna Inn, renowned for its retro elegance and kitsch, made the perfect setting for their marriage.

The venue was a “no-brainer” as it “matched the tone and aesthetic of the wedding” so much that hardly any decorations were actually needed, the couple told People.

David Morris married wife Miranda Harrison after 10 years of dating. (Source: Instagram)

The wedding was unique since the couple wanted it to be exciting and different for their 111 guests, which included well-known figures like Tana Mongeau and Rebecca Knox.

Beyond all the glamor and flash, the wedding ceremony was an intimate family affair that held great significance to Morris and his wife Harrison.

Remarkably, years into their relationship, their families uncovered a hidden relationship. The pair were both in the picture from Harrison’s fourth birthday celebration.

The reason was that Morris’ father and Harrison’s mother were coworkers in the 1990s, making the marriage feel fated.

Morris’s father took on the role of reader, and Harrison’s godmother served as the ceremony’s officiant. The couple’s beloved maltipoo, Lotus, escorted the newlyweds down the aisle, while Harrison’s nieces served as flower girls.

Harrison wore a strapless Katherine Tash gown that was influenced by historical fashion, while Morris wore a classic black and white suit and completed the look with a black bow tie.

Morris walked down the aisle to the ’70s love song, Willie Hutch’s ‘I Choose You,’ and met his bride at the altar to the hit song, ‘Yebba’s Heartbreak’ by Drake.

The pair exchanged their vows, both traditional and handwritten promises, sealed with a kiss and made their exit set to the romantic tune, ‘Kiss Me’ by Sixpence None the Richer.

At the reception, a treasured memory was honored when the pair danced for the first time to Neil Young’s ‘Harvest Moon.’ The song had a special place in their hearts as they had witnessed Young play this song at the 2016 Desert Trip music festival in Indio, California.

Miranda Harrison’s Instagram is full of wedding posts

Harrison’s Instagram has been flooded with an array of heartwarming wedding posts since she and Morris first shared the thrilling news of their wedding on October 10, 2023. 

The video showed the newlyweds in their wedding attire, posing gracefully in front of the camera, and captioned it, “Forever Morris.” 

Harrison provided several behind-the-scenes looks at her wedding day, adding to the excitement. On October 13, 2023, she posted another video, which showed her carrying a plate full of cheese.

She wrote, “When you leave enough time in your wedding day schedule to walk around with a cheese plate and approve the whole reception,” as she walked crunching the cheese.

On October 18, 2023, Harrison delighted her fans once again by sharing a video of herself dancing with her nieces, and wrote, “When your sister gives birth to your twin flame.” 

Further, Harrison posted a video featuring her best friend, Lia Dorchinez, on October 30, 2023, emphasizing the role her friend played in her journey to the altar. 

“It was honestly our wedding too… 💗 so lucky to have my best friend @liadorchinez by my side not only on my wedding day but every step of the way to the altar. there’s just no one like her,” she expressed.

Fans from worldwide congratulated the couple and expressed their well wishes, with one fan writing, “I love how much content you have from your wedding!” Another fan wrote, “MOST BEAUTIFUL COUPLE I LOVE YALL!!! CONGRATS.”

“Maybe it’s just me but like logically they look like they literally go together like they were made specifically for each other they just fit so well together face wise,” the third comment read. 

Who is David Morris’ wife Miranda Harrison?

Morris’ wife, Harrison, is a model and singer, and her presence on Instagram, has garnered her a significant following with over 135 thousand followers. 

As a model, she has collaborated with brands like Clare Bare for their Lingerie Collection. Beyond her modeling career, she has ventured into the world of music. 

Her top tracks include ‘You Don’t Know,’ ‘Rearview,’ and ‘When It Comes to Love,’ which have resonated with her growing fan base. 

Furthermore, Harrison’s recently launched her own merchandise, offering a limited edition run of pieces specially made for the 2023 Valentine’s Day Series.
