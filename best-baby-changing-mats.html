What should I look for in a baby-changing mat?

Baby changing mats need to be easy to clean, so look for one that can be wiped down using a baby-safe cleaning product. Some changing mats have machine washable covers, which helps if there's been a poonami incident.

It always helps to get a changing mat that can be folded up easily, especially if you're out and about a lot for sensory classes or midwife appointments. Some baby changing bags come with a changing mat so it saves the hassle of hunting another one down.

How many baby-changing mats do you need?

It comes down to personal preference, but it's always handy to have one changing mat in the house and one in the bag—saves at least one minute when you're trying to leave the house.

Some people might have one changing mat in the baby's room, one downstairs and one in a bag. It always helps to make sure grandparents have their own changing mats too.

Do I need a baby changing mat?

There isn't really a time you wouldn't need a baby-changing mat.

While you can get changing tables with mats built in, you will always need a changing mat when you go out. Portable and packable, it means that you don't need to worry about cold, hard floors or staining carpets—trust us, without a changing mat it will happen... in fact, it might happen with one.
