Beyonce &amp; Jay Z Break Up ALL the Time!

As Beyonce and Jay Z are drinking in the sweet success of Bey’s new album "Lemonade," a long-lost cousin has come along to give them something to choke on.

Shanica Knowles, Beyonce’s cousin, is dishing out some serious dirt about the couple’s marriage to heat magazine.

Shanica, best known for her recurring role on Hannah Montana, says Bey and Jay have split up multiple times over the years because of the rapper’s cheating, which of course was the main focus of "Lemonade."

"This has been a long process and I’ve heard they’ve had to deal with silent separations and splits over the past few years," she revealed. "…but Beyonce is praying that this album will actually save their marriage."

The actress goes on to say that Beyonce used the new album as some kind of catharsis to help her cope with her husband’s betrayal. 

"Beyonce’s been encouraged to write about the affair rumours to help her deal with it all and help her determine whether she could work through everything or had to end the marriage," she said. 

"She’s been through the intense anger and I think she’s now learning to forgive Jay."
