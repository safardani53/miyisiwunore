Sofia Vergara goes Instagram official with boyfriend Justin Saliman at family holiday party 5 months

SOFIA Vergara has gone Instagram official with her boyfriend Justin Saliman at her family holiday party just five months after her divorce.

The America's Got Talent judge split from her ex Joe Manganiello over the summer.

Sofia took to Instagram to share a photo from an intimate holiday dinner party.

She wore a silky green matching top and pants and smiled up at the camera.

Her new boyfriend Justin Saliman could be spotted sitting at the dinner table with the other guests.

Sofia's ex-husband Joe was nowhere in sight.

Justin is a successful orthopedic surgeon who has offices in both Beverly Hills and Los Angeles.

NEW MAN

An insider claimed: "Sofia has moved on from Joe and would be happy to never see him again. Her relationship with Justin has made her never look back," according to the Daily Mail.

The source added: "Justin is absolutely charming, and handsome, and has an incredible job. He isn't an actor and is very confident and she is eating it all up."

They have been spotted together on a different occasions as the new happy couple have flaunted their new love on the streets of Los Angeles.

SECOND CHANCES

Sofia was spotted on a date with Justin on October 20 in Los Angeles after her split from Joe Manganiello in July. 

The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed that Justin, too, has been through divorce, as his ex-wife Bree Turner filed to end their union on March 2, 2018, after nearly 10 years of marriage. 

The Grimm actress cited irreconcilable differences as the reason for their divorce, court papers claim.

She requested spousal support and asked the court to terminate his right to alimony while requesting they share legal and physical custody of their two young children. 

In Justin’s response on April 13, 2018, he requested spousal support from her and demanded the court terminate her right to alimony. 

On June 22, 2018, Justin was ordered to pay temporary spousal support to Bree in the amount of $4,571, as well as $3,183 in child support for their two kids. 

In their divorce judgment filed on November 20, 2019, they agreed to joint physical and legal custody of their daughter and son. 

He has been ordered to pay her $3,000 in spousal support until April 30, 2025, or until she remarries, alongside $4,000 a month until the children are 19 years old.

OUT WITH THE OLD

In July, news broke that Sofia's ex-husband Joe filed for divorce, with the couple stating: "We have made the difficult decision to divorce.

"As two people that love and care for one another very much, we politely ask for respect for our privacy at this time as we navigate this new phase of our lives.”

Two days after releasing the announcement, Joe filed for divorce from Sofia after seven years of marriage, citing “irreconcilable differences.”
