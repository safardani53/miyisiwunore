Tiwa Savage (R&amp;B Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Tiwatope Savage, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter and actor. Born in Isale Eko, she moved to London at the age of 11 to attend high school. Five years later, she began her musical career singing choir for artists like George Michael and Mary J. Blige. After joining The X Factor UK version and graduating from Berklee College of Music, Savage signed a publishing contract with Sony / ATV Music Publishing in 2009. Inspired by industry development In Nigeria music industry, Savage returned to Nigeria and signed with Mavin Record in 2012. She appeared on the 2012 compilation album of Solar Plexus. His first studio album Once Upon a Time was released on July 3, 2013. It is supported by seven singles: "Kele Kele Love", "Love Me", "Without My Heart", "Ife Wa Gbona", "Folarin", " Olorun Mi "and" Eminado ". The album was nominated for Best Album of the Year at the Nigeria Entertainment Awards 2014 and for Best R & B / Pop Album at the 2014 Headies .
