&amp; Other Stories is selling a 69 knitted jumper that looks just like Ganni's 370 designer version

IT'S officially jumper season, which means we're on the lookout for a cosy knit to team with jeans, midi skirts and chic checked trousers.

So when we found this £69 cream knit jumper that looks just like a designer version worth £370, we couldn't wait to get our hands on it.

Both styles are crew neck, with eyelet stitching and bell-shaped sleeves.

The versatile jumpers are soft cream in colour and can be teamed with pretty much anything.

Try pairing with tapered, checked trousers or a leather midi.

Or, layer over a high-neck top and team with a coloured corduroy mini skirt.

  • Eyelet knit sweater, £69, & Other Stories - buy now

While the knitted sweaters are strikingly similar, there are some subtle differences.

The designer style is a slightly looser fit with a chunkier neckline.

And while the more expensive jumper is 50 per cent wool and 50 per cent mohair, the high-street sweater is made from a combination of polyamide, acrylic, wool and mohair.

  • Ganni mohair and wool sweater, £370, My Theresa - buy now

Of course, the biggest difference is the price.

While Ganni's designer jumper would set you back £370, you can snap up & Other Stories' high-street style for just £69.
