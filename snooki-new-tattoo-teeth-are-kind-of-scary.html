New Tattoo, Teeth Are Kind of Scary

Snooki ended 2012 with a new tattoo and a new set of chompers.

Both are looking fierce and a little scary … in very different ways.

“I wanted to get this because to me it represents being a strong independent woman, and obviously I love leopards/leopard print,” she wrote of the her tattoo (below).

“The crown represents being a queen and being fabulous, and the wings represent everyone who has passed in my family,” the Jersey Shore star tells Celebuzz.

 

“Anytime I can incorporate my loved ones who have passed, I do it.”

No word if she is therefore planning a tat in honor of her dignity.

As for her new teeth? Yeah. Those are not natural-looking.

Whiter teeth look good, as we all know, but there’s something about a set that looks like it’s been bleached, painted, power-washed and encased.

Then again it’s Snooki. Probably the least absurd thing about her.

The reality star celebrated more milestones than this in the past year, of course. She got engaged to Jionni LaValle and gave birth to baby son Lorenzo.

She actually seems like a pretty good mom too. Bravo, Snook!
