Castle Season 7 Episode 7 Teaser: Dude, Where's Our Caskett?

Ready to saddle up, Castle fans?

On Castle Season 7 Episode 6, the event many have been waiting years for finally took place: Kate Beckett and Rick Castle became Husband and Wife.

Following an alternative reality created by a very fatigued Castle – in which Rick envisioned a world in which he never met Kate – our favorite author proposed to his detective fiancee. Again.

She said yes. They got married in the Hamptons with only their closest loved ones around. And we’re gonna need some Kleenex before we can talk about this future.

So, where will the ABC series go on Castle Season 7 Episode 7? To a dude ranch! For real!

 

The newlyweds will head to an Old West-style resort after learning that a murder victim may have been poisoned there.

And they’ll look pretty awesome while doing so!

Check out the above teaser to get a look at Castle as a Cowboy and Beckett as a cowgirl and then watch Castle online via the following video.
