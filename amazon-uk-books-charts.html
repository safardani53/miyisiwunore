Amazon launches first top 20 book chart for the UK - revealing 90s books are still just as popular

AMAZON has launched its first top 20 most read book chart for the UK - revealing 90s books are still being snapped up by Brits.

The bestseller list shows the most read and sold books across all formats including Kindle and Audible.

And seven of the most read fiction books this week are Harry Potter books - proving the boy wizard is still just as popular as he was more than 20 years later.

While more people listened to Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows than read it on Kindle over the past seven days.

Marian Keyes’ 90s cult classic Rachel’s Holiday appears in the 20 most read fiction books this week alongside other hits from the decade including A Game of Thrones by George R R Martin.

Amazon UK book charts top 10 most read non-fiction books this week (across digital, audio and subscription service books)

1. Becoming - by Michelle Obama

2. This is Going to Hurt - by Adam Kay

3. Sapiens - by Yuval Noah Harari

4. 12 Rules for Life - by Jordan B. Peterson

5. The Subtle Art of Not Giving A F*** - by Mark Manson

6. Can’t Hurt Me - David Goggins

7. The Secret Barrister - by the Secret Barrister

8. The Chimp Paradox - by Professor Steven Peters

9. The Book You Wish Your Parents Had Read - by Philippa Perry

10. Educated - by Tara Westover

Britain's Got Talent judge David Walliams has made an appearance in the most sold fiction books this week with The World’s Worst Teachers.

But topping the list is And So It Begins by Rachel Abbott, followed by Darkness on the Fens by Joy Ellis.

Non-fiction is still proving a popular genre - with Becoming by former First Lady Michelle Obama the most read.

While the most sold non-fiction book is The Spy and the Traitor by Ben MacIntyre.

Amazon UK book charts most sold fiction books this week (across physical, digital, audio and subscription service books)

1. And So It Begins - by Rachel Abbott

2. Darkness on the Fens - by Joy Ellis

3. The Winner - by David Badalcci

4. The World’s Worst Teachers - David Walliams

5. The Things I know - Amanda Drowse

6. The Lemon Tree Hotel - Rosanna Ley

7. I Looked Away - Jane Corey

8. Child’s Play - Angela Marsons

9. What You Did - Claire McGowan

10. The Perfect Child - Lucinda Berry

Amazon Charts is a weekly bestseller list that shares the most read and sold books across all formats on Amazon UK each week.

Their Top 20 Most Read list is the first ever bestseller list to measure which Kindle and Audible titles Amazon.co.uk customers are reading and listening to most each week.

While The Top 20 Most Sold list ranks the most books sold, pre-ordered or borrowed each week from Amazon.co.uk and Audible.co.uk in physical, ebook or audiobook formats.

Simon Johnson, Country Manager, Amazon UK Books, said: “We’re very excited to be launching Amazon Charts in the UK, giving our customers brand new insights into the most

read and most bought books across all formats in our store.

"We hope that the weekly lists will inspire readers to try something new, whether in physical, ebook or audio.”
