Ty Dolla Sign Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Ty Dolla Sign (born Tyrone William Griffin, Jr. on April 13, 1985 in Los Angeles, California) is an American hip hop and R&B singer. He released his first mixtape Beach House in 2012 after signing a deal with Atlantic Records, then in 2013, he signed to Wiz Khalifa‘s label Taylor Gang Records. He released his debut studio album Free TC in 2015.

Best Known For:
Ty Dolla Sign is best known for his hit singles “Paranoid”, “Or Nah” and “Blasé”.

Personal Life:

Ty’s father is Tyrone Griffin, a member of the funk band Lakeside. Ty can play the bass guitar, drums, guitar, keyboards and the MPC. Ty has a daughter named Jailynn Griffin.
