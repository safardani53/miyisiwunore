Back in June, Kiaya's baby daddy X'zayveon Gambrell was released from prison after four years.

Kiaya gave birth to her only child with X'zaybeon when he was already in custody on charges of receiving a stolen firearm, felony possession of a weapon/ammo (not a gun), and possession of marijuana with the intent to distribute.

According to RadarOnline, he was sentenced to five years behind bars and was denied parole in 2019 because of his "extensive criminal record, and serious nature and circumstances of offenses."

The parole board added that they had concluded he "should serve more of your sentence prior to release on parole" and "the board concludes you to be a risk to the community." 

His release date was set for January 9, 2023, but he was released early.

Chatter about his impending release was featured on Teen Mom: Young & Pregnant.
