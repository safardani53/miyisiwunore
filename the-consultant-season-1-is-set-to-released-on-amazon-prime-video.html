The Consultant Season 1 Is Set To Released On Amazon Prime Video

The Consultant season 1 is a highly anticipated and fresh new dark comedic mystery thriller series that will premiere on Amazon Prime Video on Friday, February 24, 2023, at 12:01 a.m. Eastern Time (ET). Tony Basgallop is the man behind the interesting series.

Since Prime Video released the official teaser for the first season of The Consultant, fans have been buzzing with excitement to see how the forthcoming interesting TV series will evolve. Therefore, without further ado, let’s dive right in to learn all there is to know about the series’ first season before it premieres on Amazon Video. Discover all there is to know about season 1 of The Consultant before it premieres on Amazon Video.

How to Stream The Consultant Season 1 Online?

Season 1 of the series, as previously indicated, will be published exclusively on the popular streaming platform Amazon Prime Video on Friday, February 24, 2023. The series will begin at 12:01 a.m. ET. To see the next series, viewers will simply need a membership to the popular streaming site Amazon Video.

What can we anticipate from the first season of the new series?

The first season of the show will consist of eight episodes. The official summary for the first season of the series, issued by Amazon Video, reads:

“The Consultant is a dark, comedic-thriller series about a diabolical connection between a boss and an employee. When a new consultant, Regus Patoff (Christoph Waltz), is recruited to help the App-based gaming firm CompWare enhance its business, workers face new expectations and obstacles that call everything into question… even their lives.”

The official description and video give viewers a taste of what to anticipate from the brand-new Prime Video series. By all appearances, the series will be full of thoughtful and striking events, as well as a riveting and humorous representation of the relationship between boss and employee.

It will be fascinating to see what kinds of problems the workers face following the arrival of the new consultant, Regus Patoffof, and how far they will go to preserve their lives. The audience will be taken on an unsettling and exhilarating trip as the sinister plot unfolds.

The Consultant season 1 cast

Christoph Waltz plays Regus Patoff, Brittany O’Grady plays Elaine, Nat Wolff plays Craig, Sloane Avery plays Rosie, Aimee Carrero plays Craig’s fiancée, Erin Ruth Walker plays Amy, and Michael Charles Vaccaro plays Iain.

Additional information about the series

Tony Basgallop, the series creator, has also written all eight episodes of the season. The episode was directed by Matt Shakman, Alexis Ostrander, Karyn Kusama, Dan Attias, and Charlotte Brändström. Matt Shakman directed the first episode, according to the network. Amazon Studios collaborated with MGM Television to create the series. Matt Shakman, Tony Basgallop, Christoph Waltz, Andrew Mittman, and Steve Stark are among the series’ executive producers.
