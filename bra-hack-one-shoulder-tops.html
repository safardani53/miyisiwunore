I'm a fashion guru...My simple hack will make sure your bra straps won't peek through with one-shoul

ONE-SHOULDER tops are all fun and games until your bra straps peek through ruining the look.

Fortunately, one savvy fashion lover has figured out a simple trick to make sure no one can see what colour bra you're wearing that day (although there's nothing wrong with that).

Lina Noory (@lina.noory), from Los Angeles, is a beauty lover, whose clever fashion hacks and make-up tips have amassed her an impressible fan base of over 536,000 followers on TikTok alone.

While her videos often attract a lot of attention, one recent video in particular has gone viral, racking up almost 440,000 views since being uploaded five days ago.

In the clip, the fashion expert demonstrated the few steps to take to hide your bra straps - whilst also ensuring the support is there, according to her.

The first step, she explained, is wearing a bra as you would normally but with the ''visible'' strap on the loosest setting.

This should be loose enough for you to then be able to pull it over your head on the opposite side of your shoulder, Lina said whilst wearing a black one-shoulder top in front of the camera.

Once done, simply pull your arm through and place the strap across the bra horizontally, making sure it's tucked into your bra.

''And just like that you have the support and a no-bra look,'' the expert revealed the end result.

''TAG someone who’d love this,'' read the caption alongside the video, which was liked by 17,000 TikTok users.

Some sceptical, some thankful, viewers took it to comment to tag friends and share their thoughts.

''Adds to favorites for future reference,'' a fan seemed to be impressed.

''I did this last night! Works like a charm!'' a second reviewed the hack.
