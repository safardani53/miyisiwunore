Kim Kardashian Wishes Happy Birthday to &quot;The Strongest Woman I Know&quot;

Kris Jenner turned 58 years old yesterday.

And, in honor of the occasion, the woman behind the Kim Kardashian sex tape received some serious love from the woman whose behind is featured in the Kim Kardashian sex tape.

"Happy birthday to my mom, momager, and my best friend!" Kim blogged Tuesday afternoon. "You are the strongest woman I know and you inspire me every single day. I am so thankful for you and love you more than I can ever tell you."

 

Kardashian continued with her gushing:

"I am blessed to have someone like you as a mother. you love and support so unconditionally. You are so beautiful, inside and out. I have learned so much from you, and still do."

Rather kind words, indeed.

It’s been a busy month of birthdays for this family. Kendall Jenner turned 18 on November 3 and was feted with a masquerade party, while Lamar Odom turns 34 today.

No birthday wishes have been sent to the baller from his sister-in-law, however. We somehow doubt any are coming his way.
