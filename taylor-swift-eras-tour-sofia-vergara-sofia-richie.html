Taylor Swift puts on show-stopping Eras tour for A-list crowd including Sofia Vergara, Brie Larson,

TAYLOR Swift has reached the last stop of the U.S. leg of her extremely-high-demand Eras tour and put on an electrifying performance for an A-list-filled Los Angeles audience. 

The pop star dazzled the screaming crowd in Inglewood, California for the first two shows of her sold-out SoFi Stadium run- which continues for six nights until her final bow on August 9th. 

Taylor, 33, played for the first time at the LA stadium on Thursday, August 3rd, and came out to a roaring audience of over 70,000 people. 

The music icon went through each “era” of her storied music career for the impressive three-and-a-half-hour concert that totaled 45 of her smash hits, deep cuts, and everything in between. 

She began the show with her Lover Era, the album she released in 2019, and walked out on stage in a custom Versace crystal-covered leotard with matching sparkling stiletto boots to her track Miss Americana & The Heartbreak Prince. 

Anti-Hero songstress Taylor was surrounded by dozens of backup dancers, singers, and her longtime bandmates as she flew through the story of her musical career- which first began in 2006 with the debut of her country ballad, Tim McGraw.

Following her six-song setlist of Lover, Taylor went on to perform the album that first shot her to international fame back in 2008, Fearless. 

She rocked her now-iconic sparkling fringe dress and cowboy boots as she strummed her guitar and did her signature spin to the songs she wrote as a teenager. 

The Grammy-winner then went more modern for her next display of talent and bejeweled the crowd with live renditions from Evermore, the surprise record she put out in 2020 during the pandemic, on the massive stage which took up nearly half of the NFL field. 

Her Reputation Era energized the crowd with booming hits including …Ready For It? and Look What You Made Me Do- which had 70,000 people scream at the top of their lungs “because she’s dead!”

Enchanted and Long Live, which have recently gone viral on TikTok, rounded out her Speak Now (Taylor's Version) portion of the show as she stunned in a shimmering purple-layered tulle ball gown custom designed by Nicole + Felicia Couture.  

The crowd went wild when it was time for Red, which includes her critically-acclaimed smash hit, All Too Well (10 Minute Version) (Taylor’s Version). 

“I have one more song from the Red era I would like to play for you, Los Angeles. Do you have 10 minutes to spare?” she asked the crowd after performing hits such as I Knew You Were Trouble and We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together.

Just before the heartbreak anthem, which she belts out with just her acoustic guitar, Taylor sang the cheerful party track 22- during which she gives her black fedora hat to one lucky fan from the audience. 

MAMBA MENTALITY

During her first LA show, Taylor shared an extra-special moment with Kobe Bryant’s daughter, Bianka, 6. 

Bianka flashed a massive smile as Taylor approached her on the edge of the stage to place the hat on the tot’s head and even gave her a kiss on the cheek as they shared a sweet embrace.

Taylor, who was friends with the Lakers legend before his tragic death in 2020, also invited Bianka’s mom Vanessa Bryant as well as older sister, Natalia.

The hitmaker continued the musical journey with Grammy-winner Folklore followed by her Album Of The Year-winning record 1989. 

When it came time for the highly-anticipated surprise acoustic set, in which Taylor does two different songs every night, the Pennsylvania native did not disappoint. 

On Thursday night, the songwriter performed her new Speak Now (Taylor's Version) single, I Can See You, on the acoustic guitar. 

The stadium roared with cheers as she played at her floral-painted piano for her Midnights breakup ballad, Maroon, as the second surprise song. 

On Friday, she switched it up by performing Our Song, a fan-favorite from her 2006 self-titled debut album as well as the 1989’s bonus track You Are In Love. 

After diving under the stage and “swimming” to the other side for a quick outfit change, Taylor closed her juggernaut of a concert with the Midnights album, which she released earlier this year. 

Her single Lavender Haze opened the enchanting final portion while the upbeat track, Karma, closed out the monumental 45-song concert as confetti floated all over the football stadium and all of the performers took their well-deserved bow.  

Taylor’s Eras show, which has caused a huge buzz due to how difficult it was to score the high-demand tickets, had some of Hollywood’s biggest names come see her rock out. 

Newly single Sofia Vergara, 51, attended the show on Friday with some of her closest gal pals. 

The Modern Family actress showed off her incredible figure in a bedazzled strapless crop top with matching pants. 

Despite her recent split from husband Joe Manganiello, 46, the AGT judge shared videos as she sang along to romance songs such as Love Story. 

Sofia Richie, 24, ditched husband Elliot Grainge, 29, for a date night with her girlfriends as she documented her Eras night while they all flaunted their handmade friendship bracelets- which fans commonly trade with each other in the crowd. 

The model screamed out the emotional lyrics to All Too Well as she wore a little white T-shirt, jeans and large gold hoop earrings. 

Oscar-winner Brie Larson, 33, couldn’t contain her excitement as she screamed “Taylor!” at the top of her lungs from her SoFi Stadium suite and was surrounded by other major A-list names. 

Lupita Nyong’o, Elle Fanning, Hilary Duff, Max Greenfield, Mindy Kaling, Sarah Paulson, Ashley Tisdale, Kesha, Shay Mitchell, Paula Abdul, and Sofia’s Modern Family co-star Jesse Tyler Ferguson also attended the first two nights of the LA Eras tour- with plenty more big names expected to go the remaining four shows.

Taylor wraps up the U.S. leg of her Eras tour on Wednesday, August 9 but recently added a second North American tour as well as international dates for later in 2024. 

She cannot seem to shake off the demand for her live shows, as the international dates and newly added cities also sold out within minutes of going on sale.
