Best Fancy Dress Ideas For Women 2019 | The Sun UK

FANCY DRESS is all about being someone else for a change. Have some fun with a new you for a special occasion!

Whether it’s Halloween or your friend’s random 80s party, there aren’t many chances to dress up in ladies fancy dress costume - when you get the opportunity, make the most of it and look fabulous!

Costumes bring out everyone’s inner child. You can become your favourite glamorous Hollywood persona or scare people hiding behind a mask, being totally unrecognisable.

The number one rule, though, is that you should never have to explain who you are. Like when Ross from Friends was 'the holiday armadillo’.

Heidi Klum wins Halloween every year with her impressive, no-nonsense commitment: spending hours in hair and make-up, morphing into characters like Shrek, Jessica Rabbit and even a scarily old version of herself.

But if you don't have Klum kinda funds, don't worry we've got you.

If you’ve been invited to a fancy dress party, the weeks are probably flying by and you still have no idea what to go as. But fear not, we’ve got tonnes of fun ideas.

What are the best fancy dress costumes for women?

Women's costumes are definitely an area where you can get creative if you're invited to a fancy dress party.

When you're stressed out trying to find an affordable costume for a fancy dress party, it can be tempting to resort to an unoriginal and boring costume.

Why not have some fun with it? Fancy dress is the perfect opportunity to switch up your style and step out of your comfort zone.

Whether you want to be sexy, spooky, or sophisticated, make sure you throw out that tired old cat costume and treat yourself to something you can't wait to show off.

You'll be sure to win 'best dressed' with one of our suggestions below.

1. Hippie Love fancy dress

Peace and lurve baby. Being a hippie has never looked so cool.

This ‘Willow the hippie’ costume consists of a peace medallion, bright floral flared top, matching trousers and a matching headscarf.

Straight from the flower power era, this look can double for a 70s party too. Just grab a long straight wig and some coloured lenses and you’ll feel just like a young Cher.

2. Silver Unicorn costume

Immerse yourself in fantasy and become a unicorn with Uncut - a hot unicorn.

This metallic bodycon jumpsuit is a spacey silver dream. Teamed with white fluffy leg warmer boot covers and a sequined unicorn headpiece, it’s a magical costume.

You’ll look out of this world.

3. Cher from Clueless costume

Oh my god,we are totally buggin' at this one. Now we can relive our fave 90s chick flick as Cher from Clueless.

Head to Amazon to get Alicia Silverstone’s iconic yellow suit. This fancy dress kit comes with a checked blazer and matching pleated mini, long socks and a crop top too. 

All you need now is some bubblegum and a brick phone.

4. Pop art costume

For the creative souls out there, this costume means you can become a real-life piece of art.

The cartoon-like red Liechtenstein-inspired polka dot dress is finished with a ‘pow’ speech bubble print and comes with a matching Pop Art face mask.

It’s unlikely anyone else will be wearing the same outfit as you in this unique get-up, and for under £15, it’s a steal too.

5. Snow White costume

Hi ho hi ho, it’s off to Very we go, to get this beautiful Snow White costume.

A sexy remake of the legendary Disney princess’ outfit, this satin and velvet mini dress has pretty puff sleeves, a tulle petticoat and is centred with a lustrous red bow.

It comes with a matching red headband, garter and choker but we suggest some long white socks as well to go full fairy-tale.

6. Wonder Woman costume

Now you can  be a superhero for the day thanks to Amazon

This sequinned Wonder Woman dress will dazzle at any fancy dress party.

You might want to add some red lipstick, the rest is all sorted.

7. Hospital nurse costume

This naughty nurse outfit from Ann Summers is going to make everyone you see want to feign illness, just so they can be your patient.

The white body-con shirt dress is spliced with an alluring red zip and printed with some cheekily placed hospital crosses.

Wear it solo with the matching headband, or add lashings of fake blood and an eye patch for a creepy Kill Bill-inspired Halloween outfit.

8. Trendy cowgirl costume

If you aren’t an OTT fancy dress kinda person, this cowboy hat is perfect for you.

This leather hat has stunning metal detailing on a brown leather strap which glamorises it beyond ordinary cowboy attire.

Team it with lots of denim and other costumes accessories for a stylish western ensemble. You can get lots of wear out of it in summer too, rocking boho chic festival looks: win win.
