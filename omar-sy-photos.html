Omar Sy Photos Hollywood Life

Omar Sy is a French actor. He currently stars in the Netflix series ‘Lupin,’ which is available now. He has starred in numerous French films and television series over the years. His other films include ‘X-Men: Days of Future Past,’ ‘Jurassic World,’ ‘Two Is a Family,’ ‘Transformers: The Last Knight,’ and more. A second installment has been filmed and will reportedly debut on the streaming service later in 2021. Omar has been happily married since 2007. He has 5 kids.
