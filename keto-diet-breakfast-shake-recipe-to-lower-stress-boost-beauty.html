Eat your way healthy! Simply pack your morning smoothie with a variety of nutritional powerhouses to start your day — and your diet — off right. It’s easy, just watch the video above!

 Healthy-lifestyle expert and author of the New York Times best-selling book Glow15 Naomi Whittel created a shake recipe just for Us, and it does so much more than just satisfy our tastebuds! This “magic elixir” (as Gwen Flamberg, Us Weekly’s executive editor of beauty and style, called it) combines essential greens like spinach, cucumber and parsley with the stress-busting adaptogen ashwagandha, mighty antioxidant resveratrol, anti-inflammatory black seed oil, plus creamy MCT oil to “rev up our metabolism,” according to Naomi.  

With A-list actresses including Halle Berry and Gwyneth Paltrow praising the effects of the keto diet, who wouldn’t want to get a taste of it? Former adult film star Jenna Jameson has also famously served as inspiration, as she’s documented her over-80-pound weightloss and life transformation due to a devotion to the keto diet on her Instagram account.

Naomi’s morning meal goes a step further beyond keto, combining many of the nutrients you need to feel great and get glowing! And the recipe is super simple to follow. All ingredients can be scooped up at gnc.com. Then watch the video above and follow the steps!

Naomi Whittel’s Clean & Green Smoothie Recipe:

Ingredients

1 large handful of leafy greens

½ cucumber, cubed

½ avocado

2 tablespoons parsley, chopped

Juice from ½ lemon

1 capsule ashwagandha powder (600mg) *open capsule and add to smoothie

1 capsule resveratrol (500mg) *open capsule and add to smoothie

2 tablespoons black seed oil

2 tablespoons MCT oil

1 scoop vitamin-packed superfood powder

1 cup filtered water

 Process

Add all ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth. Enjoy!
