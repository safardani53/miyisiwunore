Who Is Colleen Weidman Thompson, Chris Weidman Sister? Age &amp; Wiki

While Chris Weidman has left an indelible mark in MMA, little is known about his sister, Colleen Weidman Thompson.

Chris Weidman has garnered media attention after ESPN announced the E60 documentary, ‘Chris Weidman: The Return.’

The show documents the UFC Fighter’s return to action following a horrific leg injury.

Christopher James Weidman, born on June 17, 1984, is a prominent figure in mixed martial arts.

He made a name for himself as a professional mixed martial artist in the UFC following his debut in 2011.

Weidman’s journey has been marked by triumphs and significant accomplishments. Notably, he had a stint as the UFC Middleweight Champion.

He was a two-time NJCAA All-American and a two-time NCAA Division I All-American in wrestling. 

Arguably, his wrestling background laid the foundation for his successful career in mixed martial arts, which began in 2008.

Similarly, Weidman made his MMA professional debut in February 2009 in Louis Neglia’s Ring of Combat 23.

Weidman’s notable victory over Anderson Silva in 2013 secured him the UFC Middleweight Championship. He later defended the title three times over a two-and-a-half-year period.

Chris Weidman Sister: Meet Colleen Weidman Thompson

Colleen Weidman Thompson, Chris Weidman’s younger sister, steadfastly supports her brother’s journey through mixed martial arts.

Born as the third child to Charlie and Mary Weidman, Colleen grew up alongside her two older brothers.

Her support has been a constant in Chris Weidman’s life, both inside and outside the octagon.

Colleen Weidman Thompson: Age & Wikipedia

Colleen Weidman Thompson was born on April 1, 1988, in Baldwin, New York, making her 35 years old as of now.

In contrast to her brother’s pursuits in the realm of combat sports, Colleen has chosen a career in the healthcare system.

She currently serves as a registered nurse at Hillcrest Hospital, a role that reflects her commitment to helping others.

Growing up, Colleen attended Baldwin Senior High School, sharing her alma mater with her brother Chris.

Chris Weidman Sister Was Also An Athlete

While Chris Weidman excelled in amateur wrestling, Colleen pursued a different athletic path, showcasing her talents in volleyball.

She attended Nyack College (later known as Alliance University) from 2006 to 2010 and was an integral part of the Nyack College Women’s Volleyball Team.

Although Colleen specialized as a defensive specialist, she occasionally took on the libero role as well.

Nyack’s star athlete, Colleen, demonstrated exceptional skills in defense techniques and strategies.

Moreover, Colleen was known for her digs and often led inter-collegiate games with the most digs in the fixtures.

The National Christian College Athletic Association (NCCAA) recognized her prowess on the court.

Reportedly, she was named among the Scholar-Athletes in the AVCA NCCAA Division I Women’s Volleyball Awards for two consecutive seasons in 2008 and 2009.

Personal Life 

Colleen’s personal life intertwines with the combat sports realm. She is married to Tony Thompson, the brother of UFC fighter Stephen Thompson.

Likewise, the couple engaged on December 26, 2015, and exchanged vows on May 22, 2016.

In addition, they hosted a grand wedding ceremony in Simpsonville, SC, attended by many from Long Island.

Their family has expanded to include three children: two sons born in March 2018 and May 2022, respectively, and a daughter named Arabella Ruth Thompson, born in July 2020.

The Thompson family currently resides in Simpsonville, South Carolina, creating a home filled with the joys of family life and the shared passion for combat sports.
