I got a fine line tattoo, it was so pretty &amp; intricate but a year on it looks completely different -

A WOMAN who got an intricate fine line tattoo has revealed how hers healed after just a year - and it's not good.

Saara, was left disappointed to say the least when her detailed cathedral tattoo eventually healed.

Responding to a TikTok video asking to see how fine line tattoos look after a few years - she shared her experience.

The original video asked: "I keep seeing all of these fine line, incredibly detailed minuscule tattoos on my For You Page.

"They're so pretty, do not get me wrong but I just want to see one of those a year out, fully healed.

"I want to see it all jumbled together and mushed up and faded together, I want to see."

READ MORE BAD TATTOO STORIES

And Saara did not disappoint, in the first clip she showed her tattoo freshly inked - and the detail is incredible.

The cathedral, which was tattooed on the back of her hand features tiny line work and shading - and almost looks like a charcoal drawing.

But a year on, Saara's tattoo is blacked out with all the intricate details disappearing

She responded to the video by saying: "I win this one."

"Tattoo regret," she captioned the video.

Many people took to the comments section of the video in shock, one wrote: "My jaw dropped to the floor."

"OMG I had no idea! thank you for sharing," another commented.

Others suggested that fine line tattoos would last - but it all depends on the artist and placement of the tattoo.

One said: "Girl it‘s on your hand what did you expect?"

Another added: "I think the problem is it’s mostly that’s on a hand…"
